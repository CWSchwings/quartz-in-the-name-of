# Session 05 — Your Mom Let You Have Six Parents?

*The sea offered one more curiosity before they reached port. [[Zhelesyr]] offered several.*

---

## Fourth Encounter: The Dragon

An abnormal pulse rocked through the ship, followed by a small whirlpool drawing dead fish down into the dark. [[Thebrin Quass]], [[Caspian]], and [[Rene]] took a rowboat to investigate. Below the surface: a creature.

After some experimentation, they dangled a bundle of meat from the mast. A young blue dragon surfaced to take it.

They noted this and continued on.

## Port of Zhelesyr

Upon reaching [[Zhelesyr]], the crew was given a choice: disembark with a severance of one and a half gold and pursue whatever life they chose, or remain aboard and share in whatever wealth the voyage produced. Roughly twenty departed. New crew would need to be recruited before sailing again.

Work was found. The party took a shipping contract to [[Tir Zafiri]] once the ship was repaired. Research was done into costs for hull repairs and, eventually, a deck-mounted ballista.

## Bennett's Family

[[Bennett]] returned to her parents' smithy in [[Zhelesyr]]. She has, by most standards, an unusual family configuration — her living parents include her father **Faustus**, a burly orc smith; her mother **Mayari**, a purple-haired elf; and her parent **Inka**, bald and nonbinary. Three others are deceased.

The family's history is not simple. Bennett's parents were founders of the Ember Alliance, an uprising against the Thalorian crown, a struggle they ultimately abandoned when Bennett was on the way. That abandonment cost lives and sparked controversy within the movement. The family has been running from [[Zhelesyr]]'s reach ever since.

## Trouble in the Streets

While in port, the party moved against a group of muggers active in the harbor district. They took them alive. This was, for some members of the group, a point of principle. For others, it was the beginning of a more complicated evening.
