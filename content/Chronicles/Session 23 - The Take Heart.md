# Session 23 — The Take Heart

*They sold one ship and bought a better one. They also hired a healer, which was overdue.*

---

## The Parasites

Sweet-tasting seawater appeared around the ship. It contained parasites. **[[Varla Elarion]]** and [[Thebrin Quass]] worked together to develop a cure before the crew sickened badly.

## 30,000 Gold

The party received thirty thousand gold for a cache of gold trade bars. This was a significant windfall. They visited the **[[Gilded Lantern]]** to celebrate or conduct business.

## The Ship Sale

**[[Dorian Whiteshade]]** agreed to purchase the *[[Bleeding Heart]]* for 19,000 gold.

The party used the proceeds toward a new ship: the ***[[Take Heart]]**, purchased for 50,000 gold.

490 people showed up at the ship in the morning. Mass recruitment followed:
- [[Caspian]] and [[Pot]] recruited at a bar — 30 sailors.
- [[Bennett]] and [[Rene]] placed a guild advertisement for desperate applicants at a 2-gold signing bonus — 400 people responded.
- [[Johanna]] sought out outcasts — 60 ruffians.
- [[Johanna]] also found **[[The Guild With No Hall]]**.

[[Bennett]] recruited **[[Varla Elarion]]** personally.

[[Johanna]] sent 5 gold home to her family.

## Departure

The fleet departed [[Myrenthal]] for [[Vallumos]]. Arrival estimated in roughly a month.
