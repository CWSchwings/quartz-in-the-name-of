# Session 14 — Fogwatch Hollow

*Ports have their own complications. [[Fogwatch Hollow]] had several.*

---

## Pirates from Baragon Thalor

The *[[Bleeding Heart]]* was attacked by pirates flying colors from **[[Baragon Thalor]]**. The attack was repelled.

## Fogwatch Hollow

The ship arrived at **[[Fogwatch Hollow]]**, a port governed by **[[Arlin Malstrade]]**. His daughter is **[[Elira Malstrade]]**.

During their time in port, the crew acquired 100 doses of **Keoghtom's Ointment** — a significant medical reserve.

## The Emerald Expansion Deal

An arrangement was formalized to split the proceeds of a found ship with the **[[Emerald Expansion]]**.
