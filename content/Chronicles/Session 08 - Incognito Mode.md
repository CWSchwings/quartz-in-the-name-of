# Session 08 — Incognito Mode

*The ship arrived at [[Tir Zafiri]]'s main port under a new name: Bleeding Heart. Old names attract old problems.*

---

## Arinthal Harbor

The newly-renamed *[[Bleeding Heart]]* docked at [[Arinthal Harbor]], [[Tir Zafiri]]'s largest port. The party went to the merchant's guild to find work.

Two postings caught particular attention:

**[[Bishop]]** and **[[Pot]]** noticed a mercenary expedition to the New World. A local merchant was funding the operation, enlisting a substantial number of ships. Bishop's first instinct was to warn the local population of the New World — the same island, the same dinosaurs, the same dangers. He was talked into gathering more information first.

The party split to follow leads:

- The naval office had escort contracts, envoy work, and a job investigating ship disappearances.
- [[Johanna]]'s contact, an undercity boss named **[[Sundrul]]**, had information on the mercenary expedition — available for gold or a favor.

## The Favor

Sundrul's favor: break into the city's jail and recover a magical artifact currently in custody, awaiting collection by the [[Order of Bone]]. The party could keep any other artifacts found inside.

This was, on balance, not the most complicated job they'd been offered recently.

## Johanna's Family

[[Johanna]]'s brother **[[Edgar Bonnybluff]]** was in the [[Tir Zafiri Navy]]. He might know more about the mercenary expedition. The party visited the Bonnybluff family home.

The house was famously built on top of itself — additions and modifications in every direction, something like a structure that had decided to keep trying. [[Johanna]]'s adoptive mother **Mathilde** still took in strays of all kinds. New siblings had appeared since Johanna's last visit.

Edgar shared what he knew: the merchant had approached the navy with promises of precious metals and building resources from the New World. The navy declined. The expedition was reportedly heading toward an icy region, to deal with some kind of monster threat.

Then [[Rene]] noticed something. Edgar had purple veins.

*[[Manawracked]].*

Edgar mentioned, almost in passing, that the navy might have a salvage contract available — materials from a recent wreck. He seemed unaware of what was written across his own skin.
