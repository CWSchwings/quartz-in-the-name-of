# Session 11 — The Merrow Deal

*The sea gives back. It just charges for the service.*

---

## Elara Shadowstride

Before reaching the salvage site, the *[[Bleeding Heart]]* came upon an unnamed black vessel attacking a [[Calvatharn]] ship. [[Bennett]] made contact with the attacking ship's captain — **[[Elara Shadowstride]]** — and introduced the crew, explaining the ship was run by committee. Elara was interested. The two ships traded, netting the *Bleeding Heart* 100 gold profit. Elara parted friendly, suggesting the crew come find her once they'd proven themselves in real combat.

## The Salvage

The party arrived at the site of [[Edgar Bonnybluff]]'s shipwreck. [[Bishop]], [[Pot]], and [[Caspian]] approached the wreck and were immediately blocked — a group of Merrow held the site.

[[Bishop]] opened negotiations. [[Tomal Cray]] identified the Merrow's language as Seravaxan. [[Rene]] took over, with [[Bennett]] feeding him additional points.

**The deal struck:** The Merrow received 60 gold, the *Bleeding Heart*'s supply of sailcloth, and half the contents of the mysterious chest they'd been guarding. In exchange, the party received three cannons and the other half of the chest's contents.

The chest held **eight bars of [[Thramnium]]**.

The party argued about whether to honor the deal and whether to open the chest first. They opened it, discovered the Thramnium, and gave the Merrow two bars. The Merrow, delighted — they had expected far less — returned one bar as goodwill and, at the party's request, used their share to craft Thramnium piercings for the entire crew. They kept one-eighth of a bar for themselves.

## Return to Tir Zafiri

The *Bleeding Heart* docked back in [[Tir Zafiri]].

- [[Johanna]] delivered seven of the eight Thramnium bars to [[Edgar Bonnybluff]]. He was disappointed, but appreciative.
- [[Bennett]] delivered the thirty Giant Riding Lizards to a representative of the Tir Zafiri Army and received a receipt of goods.
- [[Bennett]] and [[Pot]] each found a Giant Riding Lizard egg. Expected to hatch around mid-autumn.

**New contracts accepted:**
- Escort a trade mission to [[Arctoriam]] via [[Myrenthal]] — 400 gold
- Transport a group of merchants to [[Myrenthal]] — 60 gold
- Transport wanted criminals to [[Zylandrom]] — 350 gold (credit with [[Sundrul]])
