# Session — The Wolf Hunt

*Somewhere between the sea and the snow, the crew found themselves hunting wolves with a nobleman's hospitality and a growing number of questions about silver.*

---

## Context

The party is ashore, staying as guests in a guesthouse associated with **[[Bastian Malivorna]]**, [[Bennett]]'s childhood friend and a nobleman of [[Durathia]]. The night prior, they fought wolves. Several unsettling details have emerged from that encounter and the days following.

## The Morning After

**[[Bennett]]** used the quiet hours to explore **Bastian**'s room while he was absent. She found a false panel in the wall. Behind it: a leather-bound book — a private diary, which she replaced — and a ring of seven or more keys with no obvious purpose. Bastian did not return to his room before dawn.

He arrived in the morning looking tired.

## Silver

The group decided to find better weapons. The wolves required silver to kill reliably. [[Rene]] expressed interest in crafting a weapon from the wolf's skeleton. The silver could be ready by the following day.

An errand to the local manor gave [[Johanna]] access to the estate's maps and allowed the group to get a rough sense of where the wolf cave might be located. They also explored the ballroom and made a creditable attempt at learning some dances. The kitchens were visited. Bastian went to see his tutors.

## The Woman with the Bracelet

Out in town, the group noticed a woman wearing a bracelet that matched one in **[[Johanna]]**'s possession. She collected bags of supplies, mounted a horse, and rode away. The local contact **Vinnie** identified her as **Sotha** — she came through every couple of days for supplies.

**[[Johanna]]** attempted to use the bracelet to observe what Sotha was doing. Instead of scrying, they *switched places*. For a moment Johanna was elsewhere and Sotha was here. Johanna caught a glimpse of **[[Pot]]** and Vinnie before the effect ended.

Later examination of the bracelet produced more questions:
- After removing it, Johanna noticed longer hair at her wrist.
- **[[Pot]]** reached into his pocket and burned himself on a coin. Silver.
- The bracelet caused itching.

The party agreed not to wear the bracelet until they understood it better.

## Silver and Pot

**[[Pot]]** being burned by silver coin was not a minor detail. He discussed it with **[[Bennett]]**, and the two of them talked through the implications — the wolf fight, the bite risk, the infection vectors. The possibility of lycanthropy was not named outright, but it sat in the room.

**[[Pot]]** went to **[[Corren Saithe]]**. Corren directed him to **[[Thebrin Quass]]**, who called him *Push* (apparently her preferred rendering of his name, which she found amusing).

Her assessment: there is no clean cure. The best option is amputation of whatever was bitten. Aconite was mentioned as a secondary option. Nobody wanted a limb removed.

## Evening Conversations

Before bed, the party scattered and compared notes:

**Bennett and Vinnie** — agreed that Bastian should be kept away from the wolf cave fight. He does not need to be in the middle of whatever happens there.

**Rene and Vinnie** — Rene shared that his own first kill had also been an animal, and that the weight of it matters. He gave Vinnie a rapier. He called it **FirstBlood**.

**Caspian and Johanna** — Caspian apologized for being too direct earlier about the bracelet. Johanna reflected that magic and the wolf transformation both represent a kind of strength she rarely feels she has access to in her own body. The appeal of both, even with the risks, is real.

**Pot and Bennett** — Pot talked about feeding **SK** (a creature being maintained aboard or nearby), noting that it becomes noticeable when Vinnie does it and they should be more careful. He also told her about the silver burning. She suggested involving Corren; Pot was skeptical of his knowledge in this area.

## Forgery Documents

The party is carrying forged identity papers for multiple nations, prepared in advance:

- **[[Calvatharn]]:** Senior Metallurgist Ha Zellpen
- **[[Myrenthal]]:** Cryptographers' Guild Deputy Head Sesklos Adalwen
- **[[Vallume]]:** Princess Cecilia Pavlya
