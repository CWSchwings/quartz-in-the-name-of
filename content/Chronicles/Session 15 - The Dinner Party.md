# Session 15 — The Dinner Party

*Someone threw a dinner. Someone repaired a ship. Two people learned to work with bone.*

---

A dinner party was held — details of guests and conversation remain in the oral record.

Ship repairs were completed during this period.

[[Rene]] received lessons in bone forging. [[Bennett]] received lessons in bone alchemy. The [[Order of Bone]] produces [[Thramnium]] by methods they keep secret, rumored to involve the bones of the [[Manawracked]]. The party is, evidently, developing their own understanding of the material.
