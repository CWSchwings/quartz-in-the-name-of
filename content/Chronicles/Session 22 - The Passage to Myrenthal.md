# Session 22 — The Passage to Myrenthal

*The sea offered storms, magnets, clouds, and a figure walking on the water. The crew declined most of them.*

---

## At Sea

The passage to [[Myrenthal]] included, in order:

- **Stormy sea.** Weathered.
- **Cloud Gomazoa.** Encountered and passed.
- **A lone figure walking on the water.** Cold. The ship escaped without engaging.
- **A magnetic field.** Avoided.
- **The *[[Torn Sail]]*.** Crossed paths. Learned about a vessel called the ***[[Enigma's Veil]]**.
- **Spell Scroll of Gate** acquired.

## Intelligence

A [[Myrenthal]] ship shared news: **[[Lyrelle Vanyara]]** — an ambassador — was recalled following a scandal. Details unclear.

More critically: a **[[Calvatharn]] blockade** was detected in the region. Arrival at Myrenthal was adjusted accordingly.

## Arrival

The *[[Bleeding Heart]]* arrived in [[Myrenthal]] and accepted a contract: transport arms and armor, due within the month.
