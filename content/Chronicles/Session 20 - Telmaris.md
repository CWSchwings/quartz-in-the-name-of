# Session 20 — Telmaris

*The sunken city. A blind bard on a raft. A Tir Zafiri ship doing something unconscionable.*

---

## The Sunken City

The party arrived at **[[Telmaris]]** and began exploring the sunken ruins.

## Sszraak

Out in open water, they came upon **[[Sszraak]]** — a blind, mute lizardfolk bard — floating alone on a raft. He was brought aboard.

## The Tir Zafiri Ship

A [[Tir Zafiri]] ship was encountered nearby. It had killed a group of Merrow. The *[[Bleeding Heart]]* intervened and saved approximately forty people from the ship.
