# Session 13 — The Open Water

*Between ports, the ocean keeps its own counsel. The party learned to listen.*

---

## Myrenthal to Wherever

Departing [[Myrenthal]], the *[[Bleeding Heart]]* headed into open water and encountered, in rough order:

**Sirens.** The crew encountered a siren encounter at sea — notable given what is known (and not known) about [[Caspian]]'s heritage.

**Forty bodies.** Floating. No obvious cause. The ships passed them.

**Humpback whales.** Observed without incident.

## Abyssalon

An entity or force named **Abyssalon** was encountered. [[Rene]] negotiated, offering two ships in exchange for the safety of the *[[Tulip Blossom]]*. The bargain was accepted.

## The Ghosts of Telmaris

The party met **[[Elyria Tidecaller]]** and the other **[[Ghosts of Telmaris]]** — travelers or spirits connected to the sunken city of [[Telmaris]]. The nature of this encounter and what was exchanged is recorded in the deeper logs.

## Navigation

The party acquired coordinates for a princess of [[Vallume]] — a location they might need later.

They also received a beacon pointing toward the **[[Tidal Obelisk]]**.
