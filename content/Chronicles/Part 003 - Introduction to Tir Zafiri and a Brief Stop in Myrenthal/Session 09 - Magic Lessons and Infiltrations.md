## New Faces

Among those liberated from [[Zhelesyr]]'s prison and the various rescued and recruited along the way, a number of lizardfolk had joined the crew of the *[[Bleeding Heart]]*. They were given names, in the fashion of the ship's company:

Bernadette, Olivia, Francesco, Arthur, Frankie, Boga, Erick, Sergio, Zachariah, Chace, Amos, Flowers, Logan, Silver, Ozzie, Jagger, Scottie, Purdy, Skipper, Darwin, Bully, Friday, Wesley, Nutmeg, Gretel, Blanche, Flake, Bosco, Beauty, Curly.

The ship's coffers at this point held 600 gold, 7 silver, 3 copper. Ongoing debts were being tracked.
