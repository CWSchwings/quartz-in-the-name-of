# Session 10 — Myrenthal Bound

*The sea was not done with them. It rarely is.*

---

## Thebrin Stays Put

When the *[[Bleeding Heart]]* passed near [[Calvatharn]] waters, [[Thebrin Quass]] requested to be let off. After discussion, the [[Captains]] declined. She was not yet free to leave.

## The Kelp and the Dragon Turtle

A rapidly growing kelp forest began attaching itself to the hull. While most of the crew worked to cut it back, [[Rene]] synthesized a poison to kill it at the root. The forest was cleared.

Not long after, the ship encountered a dead, floating dragon turtle. [[Bennett]] advised leaving it alone. They left it alone.

## Johanna and the Manawrack

[[Johanna]] had been [[Manawracked]] — the purple marks of magic exposure tracking across her skin. She lost another stage of the affliction during this passage and shed the last of it shortly after. It cleared.

How she contracted it remains an open question.

## The Giant Squid

A giant squid attacked the *Bleeding Heart*. The [[Captains]] attempted to fight it, then changed approach and lured it away with chum. It left.

## Myrenthal

The ship arrived in [[Myrenthal]].

The party enrolled the *Bleeding Heart* in the **[[Myrenthal Shipping Guild]]** for 30 gold, valid for a year. Through the guild, they accepted a contract: transport thirty Giant Riding Lizards to [[Tir Zafiri]] for 350 gold. They purchased 50 gold of insurance on the cargo.
