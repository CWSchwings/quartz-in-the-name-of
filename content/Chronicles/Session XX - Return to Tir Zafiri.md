# Session — Return to Tir Zafiri

*The sea had changed while they were ashore. [[Vallume]] and [[Tir Zafiri]] were at war.*

---

## Before Departure

**Shadowfin** remained behind at the keep to maintain communications with the criminal organization that had agreed to use it as a base — an arrangement made on [[Johanna]]'s behalf. The keep would serve as a fixed point for their contacts while the ship moved.

## The Battle in the Harbor

Pulling into [[Tir Zafiri]], the party encountered a naval engagement already in progress. [[Vallume]] ships against [[Tir Zafiri]] ships. The cause was not immediately apparent, but information was gathered quickly:

**[[Calvatharn]] and [[Tir Zafiri]] have made an alliance.** It formalized three to four months ago. No other nations are currently party to the same arrangement. [[Vallume]] and [[Darathia]] stand in opposition.

The alliance changed the political map in ways that affected every decision the party had been considering. [[Tir Zafiri]]'s relationship with the crew of the *[[Bleeding Heart]]*, never uncomplicated, became more so.

## A Name to Remember

**Dashiell Howell** — a high-ranking [[Tir Zafiri]] official who is known to be opposed to the [[Calvatharn]] alliance. A potential contact, or at minimum someone whose position is useful to understand.
