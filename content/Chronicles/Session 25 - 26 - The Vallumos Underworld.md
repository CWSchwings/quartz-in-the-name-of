# Sessions 25 & 26 — The Vallumos Underworld

*The sewers held more than they let on. The prison was educational.*

---

## Session 25: Shadowfin

[[Rene]] and [[Johanna]] tracked down **[[Shadowfin]]** through the [[Vallumos]] underworld, presenting themselves under the aliases established in [[Session 24 - Vallumos|Session 24]].

**[[Shadowfin]]** was told to seek **Aslan** at the **Stuffy Scabbard** in [[Vallumos]] if contact was needed in future.

## Session 26: Prison Break (Again)

The party split: some inside a prison, some outside.

**Outside:** Potentially magical items were stored in [[Bennett]]'s room at **[[Alaric's Harpoon]]**.

**Inside:** [[Rene]] used the alias John Burr. [[Johanna]] used the alias Alex Hamilton. They met **[[Benjamin Lucerra]]** and **[[Charles Trellan]]** while incarcerated.

[[Caspian]], [[Bishop]], and [[Pot]] went to the guard, identified the party as crew of the *Take Heart*, and had [[Rene]] and [[Johanna]] released. The exit cost 100 gold total and earned them some notoriety in Vallumos.

## The Oblex Returns

Back in the sewers: **[[Mirath the Remembered]]** attacked again and successfully absorbed additional memories. The tally at last count:
- [[Pot]]: 4 memories absorbed
- [[Caspian]]: 4 memories absorbed
- [[Bennett]]: 4 memories absorbed
