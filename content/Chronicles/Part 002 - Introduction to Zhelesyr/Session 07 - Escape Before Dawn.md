# Session 07 — Escape Before Dawn

*The fortress opened fire on them as they left. This was, by then, expected.*

---

## The Deeper Cells

[[Johanna]] and [[Caspian]] followed the missing tieflings' trail into the prison's lower levels and ran into two members of the [[Order of Bone]]. Against expectation and the odds they appeared to be working against, they won. More tieflings were freed — bullywugs and lizardfolk as well.

## The Run to the Ship

All groups moved fast and quiet toward the docks, toward *Khalvyn's Sacrifice*. [[Bennett]] stopped at home to tell her parents she was leaving again. She was stopped on the dock by guards already aware of the jailbreak. She talked her way through — drunken crewmates, nothing to see.

They cast off.

The fortress answered with cannon fire. The party returned fire on a ship preparing to pursue them and damaged its mast enough to buy time. Once in open water, repair work began on their own mast and sails, which had taken hits.

## The Escape

Discussion of options: hide in a coastal inlet, fight their pursuers, use magic. The last carried risk that many aboard were reluctant to accept, but [[Corren Saithe]] and one of the newly-freed tieflings were willing to act. They cast Darkness over the ship at the horizon. In the confusion, their pursuers changed course. The *Khalvyn's Sacrifice* turned toward [[Tir Zafiri]].

## Interlude: The Smuggler

A dead-in-the-water vessel on the way out of [[Zhelesyr]] waters. The captain was evasive about their situation. Smugglers, probably. They needed sail cloth and timber. They had gold.

[[Johanna]] worked the price up to five hundred gold total. The party provided the supplies. The smuggler's cannons stayed where they were, despite some admiring looks from the party.

## Landfall: Outer Tir Zafiri

They put into one of the outlying islands around [[Tir Zafiri]]. [[Bennett]] and [[Rene]] went ashore at a local inn. The townsfolk avoided them. The barkeep gave nothing.

Back on the ship, [[Johanna]] and [[Caspian]] explained: [[Tir Zafiri]] pressgangs their navy from the locals. An unaffiliated armed ship in harbor looks like a threat. The locals were probably protecting themselves.

This opened a longer conversation. How [[Johanna]] and [[Caspian]] had come to serve in the [[Tir Zafiri]] navy emerged: both had been given a choice between prison and service. When it came out that their former first mate and captain had been skimming wages into a "savings program" without consent, [[Bennett]] named it plainly. That was slavery.

[[Caspian]] sat with that for a while.
