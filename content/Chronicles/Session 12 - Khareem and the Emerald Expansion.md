# Session 12 — Khareem and the Emerald Expansion

*New port. New players. Someone was looking at their ship.*

---

## Khareem

The *[[Bleeding Heart]]* arrived in [[Khareem]], [[Tir Zafiri]]'s largest port city. Eight shipwrecks floated in nearby waters. The party gave [[Corren Saithe]] ten Thramnium piercings.

The *[[Blue Leviathan]]* was spotted and avoided.

## The Emerald Expansion

At the **Restful Skies** inn, the party met **[[Zara Firebeard]]** of the **[[Emerald Expansion]]** — a multi-captain shipping enterprise operating in the region. She questioned the crew about their experience. They kept their specific history vague.

**[[Eliath Goldwhisper]]** was also present. He caught [[Rene]] in a lie but accepted the crew's involvement anyway.

## Dorian Whiteshade

[[Pot]] spotted **[[Dorian Whiteshade]]** examining the *Bleeding Heart* up close. He followed Dorian back to his own ship, the *[[Silvered Edge]]*.

## Shadowfin

[[Rene]] learned from [[Edgar Bonnybluff]] about a smuggler operating in the region — **[[Shadowfin]]**. A contact worth knowing.

## Departure for Myrenthal

The party restocked the ship for 70 gold via [[Sundrul]] and set sail for [[Myrenthal]].
