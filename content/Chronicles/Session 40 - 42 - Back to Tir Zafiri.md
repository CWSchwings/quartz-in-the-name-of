# Sessions 40–42 — Back to Tir Zafiri

*Sunken Vallumite ships. A war in the harbor. Thebrin's list. Bennett is knighted.*

---

## Session 40 — Tir Zafiri

Arriving back in [[Tir Zafiri]], the party found sunken [[Vallume]] ships off the coast. The naval war between [[Vallume]] and the [[Calvatharn]]-[[Tir Zafiri]] alliance was visible in the harbor.

In **[[Arinthal Harbor]]**, two notable ships were docked:
- ***Knot Working*** — captained by **Jibril Osric**, a sloop
- ***Elegance of Spirit*** — a [[Tir Zafiri]] ship, captained by **Tajra Kecskeméti**, jaguar leonine crew, frigate

The party contacted **[[Dashiell Howell]]** — the high-ranking [[Tir Zafiri]] official who dissents against the [[Calvatharn]] alliance. The **Mandic family** is identified as leading the coalition that voted to join [[Calvatharn]].

[[Johanna]] and [[Bishop]] went to the thieves' guild, offering warehouse space for information. A contact will follow.

[[Thebrin Quass]] provided Vinnie with a list of [[Calvatharn]] scientists she has grievances with — some professional, some deeply personal. This list may become a mission set.

## Session 41

**[[Shadowfin]]** was left at the keep to manage criminal operations. He told [[Rene]] to seek **Aslan** at the **Stuffy Scabbard** in [[Vallumos]] for future contact.

**Bennett is knighted** by **Affraic Malivorna** — [[Bastian Malivorna]]'s family. The circumstances and implications are significant.

## Session 42

[[Rene]] and [[Bennett]] went to speak with **[[Petra Bonnybluff]]** — [[Johanna]]'s younger sister.

**King of [[Tir Zafiri]]:** Anvar Yordan.

The Tir Zafiri underworld in Arinthal Harbor includes five notable figures:
1. **Lady Varissa "The Whispercloak" Darnel** — half-elf, espionage
2. **"Iron-Tooth" Garric Thane** — dwarf, smuggling and artifact recovery, iron teeth from interrogation
3. **Silk-Finger Nali** — human, pickpocketing and infiltration
4. **The Jackal Prince** — unknown race, possibly shapeshifter, assassinations
5. **Old Mother Quent** — gnome, poison and information
