# Sessions 28 & 29 — Leaving Vallume

*Ships in the dark. Enormous hands. A dinner of captains. A very specific theft.*

---

## Enormous Hands

Out at sea, the [[Captains]] encountered enormous hands of water that reached up and seized ships. Three vessels were taken. The *[[Take Heart]]* escaped.

## The *Sunny Palm* and the *Death of Ronnic*

The party came upon the ***[[Sunny Palm]]*** — a [[Tir Zafiri]] merchant ship — being raided by the ***[[Death of Ronnic]]***. Outcome unrecorded.

## Vallumos to Myrenthal (Vaelcor's Reach)

The party arrived in [[Myrenthal]], specifically at **Vaelcor's Reach**. Notable contacts there:
- **[[Sereth Kall]]**, the Veilblade
- **[[Saedrien Moth]]**

They learned that **[[Myara Varris]]** — the individual being sought by the [[Calvatharn]] blockade — was not found. She is hiding in [[Zhelesyr]].

## The Dinner of Captains

The [[Captains]] hosted a dinner with nearby ship captains, including **[[Doran Corvath]]**. Rumors exchanged:

- **Doran Corvath** told of Captain Hadrin Keeliss of the *Golden Horse*, abducted along with three other ships by air-elemental hands and forced to fight. The *Golden Horse* won. Keeliss retired without a word.
- **[[Tir Zafiri]]**'s new shipbuilding program put them into debt.
- **[[Pres Afron]]** in [[Zhelesyr]] was taken over by the undead.
- **[[Vallume]]** is recruiting soldiers from [[Zylandrom]].

## The Theft

During the dinner, [[Johanna]] stole maps of southern [[Zylandrom]] from the ***Fairwind's Grace*** and planted evidence framing Doran Corvath of theft from a [[Myrenthal]] official.

## Vorsa Vorn

In Vaelcor's Reach, the party found **Vorsa Vorn** running a brutal boot camp for his prospective crew. He proved impossible to reason with. [[Bennett]] incited a mob. [[Caspian]] escalated the violence. [[Rene]] killed Vorsa Vorn. [[Caspian]], [[Bennett]], and Vinnie were arrested. [[Rene]] escaped. Vinnie was released. Caspian's charges were dropped. Bennett paid a 20-gold fine.
