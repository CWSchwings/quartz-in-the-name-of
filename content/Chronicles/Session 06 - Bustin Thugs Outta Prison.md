# Session 06 — Bustin' Thugs? Outta Prison!

*The tiefling gang had good darkvision. The party did not. The evening went sideways from there.*

---

## The Failed Ambush

The party attempted the old "damsel in distress" trick to draw out the tiefling gang operating in [[Zhelesyr]]'s harbor district. The tieflings spotted the ambush in the dark and most of them ran. The boss stayed to fight. She went down.

Half the crew immediately began having a crisis about turning her in. The other half pointed out they had an unconscious tiefling and a decision to make.

They took her to the ship.

## Good Cop, Bad Cop

Morning brought interrogation. [[Bennett]] played the reasonable one. [[Caspian]] played the other kind.

What they learned:

- **The [[Order of Bone]]** has a presence in [[Zhelesyr]]. **Jareth Varcryn** is the local head.
- **Thaylord, Captain of the Guard**, is working in concert with the Order. He has been filling the city's prison with people suspected of magic.
- **Governor Elrik Thelorian** has his hand in the arrangement as well. He has been ordering roundups and accepting payment to keep certain people from being handed over to the Order.
- The imprisoned include not just magic users but members of species the Order has decided to classify as a threat — tieflings, bullywugs, lizardfolk.

The boss tiefling had people in that prison.

## The First Secret

In the course of this conversation, something long avoided came out.

**[[Rene]] had been a member of the [[Order of Bone]].**

He was on the run from them. That was why he had been so insistent on the ship, why he reacted to any mention of the Order the way he did, why he had offered no explanations anyone found satisfying. He offered a partial one now.

## The Prison

The party decided to break the tieflings out.

They got inside without too much disruption. When a guard moved to alert Thaylord, [[Pot]] moved to intercept — and failed. The guard blew his horn. [[Pot]] then blew his own horn, a creative if unconvincing attempt to imply he had done it first on purpose. Some of the guards were persuaded to step aside. The prisoners were loaded into carriages.

Except for ten or so tieflings. Missing from the general population. Somewhere deeper in.

[[Johanna]] and [[Caspian]] went looking.
