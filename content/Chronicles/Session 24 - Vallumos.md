# Session 24 — Vallumos

*The capital of [[Vallume]]. Sewers. An Oblex. Someone slept with the bartender.*

---

## Arrival

The *[[Take Heart]]* arrived in **[[Vallumos]]**, capital of [[Vallume]]. The party met **[[Lucius Stormrider]]**, dockmaster, and provided the ship's new name.

Spires of rock erupted from the ocean during the approach. The party noted them and continued.

A [[Myrenthal]] ship was passed during the voyage; it had lost a crewman overboard. The *Take Heart* did not stop.

## Split Missions

The party divided to pursue leads:

- **[[Rene]] and [[Johanna]]** went to find [[Shadowfin]], using aliases: Laurentus Cullus and Fakus Namus respectively. They identified themselves as crew of the *Take Heart* to the city guard, claiming to know Shadowfin as "the Courier," working a fencing arrangement. They claimed Zhelesyr origins.
- **[[Bennett]]** went to the post office, then records.
- **[[Bishop]]** on separate business.
- **[[Caspian]]** to the barracks.
- **[[Pot]], [[Johanna]], and [[Bishop]]** went into the **[[Vallumos Sewers]]**.

## The Oblex

In the sewers, the party encountered **[[Mirath the Remembered]]** — an Oblex, a creature that absorbs memories from victims. It attacked and fled after absorbing memories from [[Pot]], [[Caspian]], and [[Bennett]].

The creature also attacked Gelatinous Cubes in the sewers.

## The Bartender

[[Bennett]] stayed at **[[Alaric's Harpoon]]** and slept with **[[Bran]]**, the bartender.

## Bishop Uses Magic

During a pirate attack at sea, [[Bishop]] deployed an arcane cannon. The recently recruited [[Myrenthal]] crew witnessed this. A confrontation ensued. This will not simply go away.
