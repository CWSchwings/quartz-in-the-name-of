Location: [[Arinthal Harbor]]

The [[Bleeding Heart]] arrives back in [[Arinthal Harbor]]. [[Johanna]] turns over 7 bars of [[Thramnium]] to [[Edgar Bonnybluff]] of the original 8. He is disappointed, but appreciative. [[Bennett]] delivers 30 Giant Riding Lizards to a representative of the [[Tir Zafiri]] Army. She receives a Receipt of Goods.

The party seeks out new missions. They accept the following:  

Escort a trade mission to [[Zylandrom]] by way of [[Myrenthal]] - 400 GP  

Transport a group of merchants to [[Pantelis]] - 60 GP 

Transport wanted criminals to [[Zylandrom]] - 350 GP (Credit with [[Sundrul]])

[[Bennett]] and [[Pot]] each found a Giant Riding Lizard egg. They are expected to hatch on [[7021-11-15]].

Still in [[Arinthal Harbor]] the [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Bleeding Heart]] go to check on rumors of [[Laurent Castillon]]'s wealth. They discover that rumors of his bankruptcy are circulating, but not widespread.  
They go to the [[Restful Skies]] to meet the [[Emerald Expansion]]. They meet [[Zara Firebeard]]. She questions them about their experience. They don't mention any specific expeditions.  

They speak to [[Eliath Goldwhisper]]. He catches [[Rene]] in a lie but still accepts the crew.  

[[Bishop]], [[Pot]], [[Rene]], and [[Bennett]] got to get spicy food.

[[Bishop]] asks [[Bennett]] where she learned to do magic.

The party catches [[Dorian Whiteshade]] checking out the ship. Pot follows him back.
