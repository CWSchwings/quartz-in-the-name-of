Location: [[Open Ocean]]

The [[Bleeding Heart]] arrives at the site of [[Edgar Bonnybluff]]'s shipwreck, [[Bennett]] finding the ship after some searching. [[Bishop]], [[Pot]], and [[Caspian]] approach the shipwreck but are stopped when a group of merrow attack. [[Bishop]] is able to initiate a negotiation. [[Tomal Cray]] is enlisted to speak with the merrow and reveals they speak [[Seravax|Seravaxan]], after which [[Rene]] takes over speaking to them on the party's behalf. [[Rene]] (and [[Bennett]] through [[Rene]]) negotiate a settlement. The merrow negotiate for 60 GP, the [[Bleeding Heart]]'s supply of sailcloth, and half of the contents of [[Edgar Bonnybluff]]'s mysterious chest, in exchange for 3 cannons and the other half of the contents of the chest. The merrow produce the chest and cannons for the party.

Once back on the [[Bleeding Heart]] with cannons and chest, the [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Bleeding Heart]] argue about whether they should fulfill the deal with the merrow and whether they should open the chest. They open the chest and find 8 bars of [[Thramnium]]. They decide to give the merrow 2. The merrow are overjoyed having expected much less, and return one bar to the [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Bleeding Heart]] and, at the [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Bleeding Heart]] urging, transmute 7/8 of a bar into [[Thramnium Jewelry]]s for the party, having kept the other 1/8 for themselves.
