The[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]] arrives at the site of[[Characters/Edgar Bonnybluff]]]'s shipwreck,[[Characters/Bennett]]] finding the ship after some searching.[[Characters/Bishop]]],[[Characters/Pot]]], and[[Characters/Caspian]]] approach the shipwreck but are stopped when a group of [[Merrow]] attack.[[Characters/Bishop]]] is able to initiate a negotiation.
[[Characters/Tomal Cray]]] is enlisted to speak with the [[Merrow]] and reveals they speak [[Seravaxan]], after which[[Characters/Rene]]] takes over speaking to them on the party's behalf.[[Characters/Rene]]] (and[[Characters/Bennett]]] through[[Characters/Rene]]]) negotiate a settlement. The [[Merrow]] negotiate for 60 [[Gp]], the[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]]'s supply of [[Sailcloth]], and half of the contents of[[Characters/Edgar Bonnybluff]]]'s mysterious chest, in exchange for 3 [[Cannon]]s and the other half of the contents of the chest. The [[Merrow]] produce the chest and cannons for the party.

Once back on the[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]] with cannons and chest, the [[Captains]] argue about whether they should fulfill the deal with the [[Merrow]] and whether they should open the chest. They open the chest and find 8 [[Bar Of Thramnium]]s. They decide to give the [[Merrow]] 2. The [[Merrow]] are overjoyed having expected much less, and return one bar to the [[Captains]] and, at the [[Captains]] urging, transmute 7/8 of a bar into [[Thramnium Piercing]]s for the party, having kept the other 1/8 for themselves.
