Location: [[Open Ocean]] near [[Myrenthal]]

The [[Bleeding Heart]] happens upon the [[Torn Sail]] during its attack on a [[Calvatharn|Calvatharnian]] ship. [[Bennett]] introduces the [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Bleeding Heart]] to [[Elara Shadowstride]], learning her name, and explains that the [[Bleeding Heart]] is run by committee. [[Elara]] is happy to trade for some supplies, netting the[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]] 100 [[Gp]] in profit. The ships part ways friendly, and [[Elara]] suggests that the[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]] can come find her for work once proven in combat.
