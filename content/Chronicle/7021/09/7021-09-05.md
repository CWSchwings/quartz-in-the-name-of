Location: [[Pantelis]]

The [[Bleeding Heart]] arrives in [[Pantelis]].

[[Lyrelle Vanyara]] departs. 

[[Bennett]], [[Johanna]], and [[Pot]] enroll the [[Bleeding Heart]] in the [[Myrenthal Shipping Guild]] until [[7022-10-05]]" for 30 GP. With their new guild membership, they also accept a job to transport 30 giant riding lizards to [[Arinthal Harbor]] for 350 GP. They buy 50 GP of insurance for the job.
