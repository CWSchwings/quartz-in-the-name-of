Location: [[Open Ocean]]

The [[Bleeding Heart|Khalvyn's Sacrifice]] encountered abnormal pulses of force through the water that rock the ship, followed by a whirlpool that seems to vaccum up the dead fish caused by the pulse.

[[Thebrin Quass]] wanted to investigate, and after luring the source of the whirlpool to jump out of that water, it is revealed to be a Young Blue Dragon. It eventually leaves the ship in peace.
