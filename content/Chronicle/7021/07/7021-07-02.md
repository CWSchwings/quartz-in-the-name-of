The future captains wake up in the morning, cross the river, deciding to head to highest point they can find on the island as a lookout point.   
Run into small pack of [[Frilled Deathspitter]]s eating a dead pirate along the way. Attempt to ambush them, doesn’t go exactly as planned.[[Characters/Pot]]],[[Characters/Bennett]]], and[[Characters/Rene]]] all drop during the fight. Scare the last couple [[Frilled Deathspitter]]s away.  
Loot: [[Leather Armor]] [[Dagger]], [[Scimitar]], [[Tinderbox]], 10 [[Bullet]]s, 10 [[GP]]

The future [[Captains]] continue to head uphill to scout the rest of the [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]]. They find lots of [[Aerosaur]]s about and decide it's best not to climb to the tippy top of the mountain where they nest.  
To the East they see large sandflats.  
To the West they see thick jungle.  
To the North they see more jungle leading to ship anchored off-shore. They can’t see who they are from this distance.  
They decide to climb back down the mountain and sleep in the jungle, safe from [[Aerosaur]]s.  [[Characters/Pot]]] and[[Characters/Rene]]] search for wood, stumbling into a small pack of [[Deinonychus]]. Both are brought down before[[Characters/Caspian]]] and[[Characters/Bennett]]] can reach them and stabilize.  
They eat a late-night meal after scavenging for wood in the dark.
