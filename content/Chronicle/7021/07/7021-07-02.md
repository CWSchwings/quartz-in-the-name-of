Location: [[Calvatharn Dig Site 1187|Unknown Dinosaur Island]]

The [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Most Recent Castaways of the Unknown Dinosaur Island]] wake up in the morning and cross the river, deciding to head to highest point they can find on the island as a lookout point.

They run into small pack of Frilled Deathspitters eating a dead pirate along the way. They attempt to ambush them, doesn’t go exactly as planned. [[Pot]], [[Bennett]], and [[Rene]] all drop during the fight. But they succeed in scaring the last couple Frilled Deathspitters away. They gather from the body of the pirate Leather Armor, a Dagger, a Scimitar, a Tinderbox, 10 Bullets, and 10 GP.

They continue to head uphill to scout the rest of the island. They find lots of Aerosaurs about and decide it's best not to climb to the tippy top of the mountain where they nest.  To the East they see large sandflats.  To the West they see thick jungle.  To the North they see more jungle leading to ship anchored off-shore. They can’t see who they are from this distance. 

They decide to climb back down the mountain and sleep in the jungle, safe from Aerosaurs.  [[Pot]] and [[Rene]] search for wood, stumbling into a small pack of Deinonychus. Both are brought down before [[Caspian]] and [[Bennett]] can reach them but survive the encounter.

They eat a late-night meal after scavenging for wood in the dark.
