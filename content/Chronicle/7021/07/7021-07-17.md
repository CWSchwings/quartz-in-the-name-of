[[Captains]] happens upon the[[Ships/Moons' Threnody]]].[[Characters/Rene]]] attempts to convince the [[Captains]] to sink the ship, but curiousity wins out. The [[Captains]] explore the ship and rescue[[Characters/Corren Saithe]]] from onboard. Everyone on the[[Ships/Moons' Threnody]]] had become[[Conditions/Manawracked]]]. The [[Captains]] finds that[[Characters/Thalia Stormrider]]] had used the [[Well of Three Moons]] on herself to transform herself into a [[Constellate Archer]].[[Characters/Rene]]] opens the [[Well of Three Moons]] but does not step inside.
