Location: [[Moons' Threnody]], [[Open Ocean]]

The [[Bleeding Heart|Khalvyn's Sacrifice]] happens upon the [[Moons' Threnody]]. [[Rene]] attempts to convince the [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Khalvyn's Sacrifice]] to sink the [[Moons' Threnody]], but curiosity wins out. The [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Khalvyn's Sacrifice]] explore the ship and rescue [[Corren Saithe]] from onboard. Everyone on the [[Moons' Threnody]] had become [[Manawrack|Manawracked]]. The [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Khalvyn's Sacrifice]] find that [[Thalia Stormrider]] had used the [[Well of Three Moons]] on herself to transform herself into a being of stars. [[Rene]] opens the [[Well of Three Moons]] but does not step inside.
