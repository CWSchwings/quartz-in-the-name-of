The [[Bleeding Heart]] happens upon the [[Unnamed Black Vessel Near Myrenthal]] during its attack on a [[Calvatharn]] ship. [[Bennett]] introduces the [[Captains]] to [[Elara]], learning her name, and explains that the [[Bleeding Heart]] is run by committee. [[Elara]] is happy to trade for some supplies, netting the [[Bleeding Heart]] 100 [[Gp]] in profit. The ships part ways friendly, and [[Elara]] suggests that the [[Bleeding Heart]] can come find her for work once proven in combat.
