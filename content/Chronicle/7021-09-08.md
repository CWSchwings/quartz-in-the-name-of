The[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]] happens upon the [[Unnamed Black Vessel Near Myrenthal]] during its attack on a[[Locations/Calvatharn]]] ship.[[Characters/Bennett]]] introduces the [[Captains]] to [[Elara]], learning her name, and explains that the[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]] is run by committee. [[Elara]] is happy to trade for some supplies, netting the[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]] 100 [[Gp]] in profit. The ships part ways friendly, and [[Elara]] suggests that the[[Ships/Bleeding Heart]]] can come find her for work once proven in combat.
