[[Captains]] happens upon the [[Moons' Threnody]]. [[Rene]] attempts to convince the [[Captains]] to sink the ship, but curiousity wins out. The [[Captains]] explore the ship and rescue [[Corren Saithe]] from onboard. Everyone on the [[Moons' Threnody]] had become [[Manawracked]]. The [[Captains]] finds that [[Thalia Stormrider]] had used the [[Well of Three Moons]] on herself to transform herself into a [[Constellate Archer]]. [[Rene]] opens the [[Well of Three Moons]] but does not step inside.
