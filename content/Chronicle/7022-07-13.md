[[Anvar Yordan]] King of [[Locations/Tir Zafiri]]

1. Lady Varissa "The Whispercloak" Darnel

Race: Half-Elf  
Specialty: Espionage and intelligence  
Personality: Smooth, unflappable, and almost preternaturally calm. She prefers secrets as her currency and rarely raises her voice — her whispers carry power enough.  
Signature Style: Operates from behind veils and intermediaries. No one’s quite sure what she truly looks like.  
Rumor: Claims she knows the real name of every noble in the capital — and uses that knowledge to hold their pursestrings.  
2. "Iron-Tooth" Garric Thane

Race: Dwarf  
Specialty: Smuggling, illegal trade, and artifact recovery  
Personality: Gruff but pragmatic. He runs his crew like a business syndicate, not a rabble.  
Mannerisms: Constantly chews something — sometimes tobacco, sometimes gemstones.  
Rumor: His real teeth were crushed during an interrogation years ago; he replaced them with enchanted iron fangs that can bite through locks.  
3. Silk-Finger Nali

Race: Human  
Specialty: Pickpocketing, infiltration, urban performance  
Personality: Flashy, charming, and always loves an audience. If she weren’t a thief, she’d be a stage star.  
Appearance: Dresses in fine silks that billow dramatically, despite their impracticality.  
Rumor: Keeps a secret ledger of every noble she’s ever robbed — and how much they paid her to keep it quiet afterward.  
4. The Jackal Prince

Race: Unknown (possibly shapeshifter)  
Specialty: Assassinations and disappearances  
Personality: Cold, calculating, believes in balance — the coin for blood must be equal to the weight of the soul taken.  
Symbol: A jackal’s pawprint scorched into parchment left at the scene.  
Rumor: Only ever works contracts delivered before dawn, and none who have seen his true form live long enough to describe it.  
5. Old Mother Quent

Race: Gnome  
Specialty: Poison crafting and information brokerage  
Personality: Sweet as honey on the outside, venomous beneath. Speaks in grandmotherly endearments while dosing opponents with truth serums.  
Quirk: Keeps a collection of "pets" — venomous insects in little glass bottles.  
Rumor: The entire thieves’ guild network owes her at least one favor, whether they know it or not.
