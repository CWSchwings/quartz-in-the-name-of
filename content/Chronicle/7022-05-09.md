all and sun‑burned, with weather‑creased features and sea‑green eyes that never seem to rest; a constant glint of mirth at the edge of fatigue. Idealist and obsessive in equal measure. Alaric believes there is _always_ something beyond the next storm—a new island, a lost route, a forgotten civilization—and he’ll wager everything on that faith. To crew he’s father, friend, and tyrant all at once: fair in punishment, reckless in command, generous in rum.

Bards claim he once sailed straight through an aether‑tempest to rescue a scholar trapped in the cyclone’s eye. Scholars claim he delivered manuscripts thought drowned for centuries. Pirates claim he talks to the wind itself; that it _answers_.

Keeps a faded brass sextant with an empty gem‑socket. Says the missing gem fell from the sky, and until he finds where it landed, his voyage isn’t over.
