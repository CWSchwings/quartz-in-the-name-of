[[Pot]] returns with the shipwrecked people and [[Captains]] plot how to overtake the remaining [[Calvatharn]] camp.  
Plans discussed are to gather the remaining commanders and blow them all up together, sneak aboard the ship without alerting the camp, or cause start spreading information of a rebellion and get more common soldiers on [[Captains]]' side.  
The explosion plan is selected.  
[[Captains]] split into three teams and gather supplies to create [[Fire Arrow]]s to ignite the gun powder barrels.  
⅔ of the party end up running into various dinosaurs on the way and get pretty hurt before the battle.  
[[Bishop]], [[Johanna]], and [[Caspian]] break off to hide the barrels around the central meeting tent before the morning commanders meeting.  
[[Rene]], [[Bennett]], and [[Pot]] go to sabotage the long guns.  
The plan goes relatively smoothly, injuring most of the commanders and kicking off a chaotic rebellion.  
Many common soldiers are confused and about half end up fighting [[Captains]].  
Eventually [[Captains]] take the camp.  
Flares are sent up from the ship, they expect some kind of coded answer.  
[[Captains]] answer wrong and the ship begins to set off.  
[[Pot]] swims out to the ship first and apprehends the scientist on board, [[Thebrin Quass]].  
A long discussion ensues about what to do with [[Thebrin Quass]]. [[Rene]] wants to kill her, [[Bishop]] just wants her off the ship, others think [[Crew]] could use a scientist.  
[[Thebrin Quass]] ends up staying aboard.  
[[Captains]] spend a week getting the ship prepared to depart and leave [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]] behind.
