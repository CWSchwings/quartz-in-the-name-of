Location: [[Vallumos]]

[[Rene]] and [[Johanna]] went to see [[Shadowfin]] in jail. They introduced themselves to the city guard as [[Rene|Laurentus Cullus]] and [[Johanna|Fakus Namus]]. They claimed to be from the [[Take Heart|Take Heart]] and said they had business with [[Shadowfin]]. They were questioned and claimed that they knew him as the [[Shadowfin|Courier]]. They claimed he was to help them fence supplies on the ship and acquire supplies to sell elsewhere. They claimed they didn't meet [[Shadowfin]] directly, but sent him money by post into [[Zylandrom]]. They claimed to be from [[Zhelesyr]].

[[Bennett]] went to the post office and then the hall of records. 

[[Bishop]] and [[Caspian]] went to the barracks.

In the prison, [[Rene]] gave the new fake name [[Rene|John Burr]] and [[Johanna]] gave the fake name [[Johanna|Alex Hamilton]]. They met [[Benjamin Lucerra]] and [[Charles Trellan]].

Outside of the prison, [[Caspian]], [[Bishop]], and [[Pot]] left all their potentially magical items in [[Bennett]]'s room of [[Alaric's Harpoon]], then went to the prison and identified themselves as being from the [[Take Heart]]. They got [[Johanna]] and [[Rene]] out with a 100 GP fine. 

The [[Captains of the Broken Heart|Captains of the Take Heart]] traveled into the sewers and attacked the gelatinous cubes and [[SK]] there.
