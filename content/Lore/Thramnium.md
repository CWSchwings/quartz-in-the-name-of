# Thramnium

**Origin:** [[Order of Bone]] — [[Order of Marrow-Bleeding]] sub-sect  
**Type:** Rare metal

---

Thramnium is an exceptionally strong and resilient metal. Its weight-for-weight strength exceeds common materials, but its true value lies in a secondary property: it resists magical corruption. A thramnium object can absorb a cumulative amount of magical contamination over its lifetime without ill effect — a property of enormous practical value in a world where the [[Manawracked]] walk and uncontrolled magic spreads like disease.

## Manufacture

Thramnium's creation process is a closely guarded secret. It is fabricated exclusively by the [[Order of Bone]], and within the Order, only the [[Order of Marrow-Bleeding]] are taught the technique. No outside smith or artificer is known to have replicated it.

The name has given rise to a persistent rumor among Order members: that thramnium is crafted, at least in part, from the bones of dead Manawracked. No one has confirmed this. No one has denied it either.

## Significance

Weapons and armor of thramnium appear primarily in the hands of the Order and those it supplies. Finding a piece in the wild suggests a serious connection to the Order — or that something went very wrong for whoever last carried it.
