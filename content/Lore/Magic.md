# Magic

*Magic is a disease. Or it was made into one. The distinction matters to very few people who have to live with the consequences either way.*

---

## The Manawaxing

Between the years 6089 and 6091 of the [[Thauviathun]] calendar, the nature of magic changed fundamentally. This period is called the **Manawaxing**.

Before: magic was extremely difficult. Years of study and thousands of platinum worth of resources went into even trivial effects. A spell to sharpen a sword required dedicated scholars with access to rare materials. Magic was weak, but it was predictable. It did what you asked it to do.

During: magic reached a brief balance of safety and usability that allowed those who embraced it to reshape the world. Power structures that had existed before broke down.

After: magic became easy. Anyone with some effort could learn it. Spells could be cast with minimal preparation. And magic became dangerous. Casters and those around them began to become [[Manawracked]].

No one has ever proven why the change occurred. People have adapted. Some accept it. Some work to understand it and reverse it. Some work to push it further.

## Manawrack

**[[Manawracked]]** is the condition that results from uncontrolled magic exposure. Symptoms include glowing purple marks on the body. It affects mental faculties while temporarily enhancing physical ones. Duration ranges from one day to three months depending on the magical cause. Multiple simultaneous cases extend the duration.

**Transmission:** casting uncontrolled magic, proximity to uncontrolled casting, contact with Manawracked individuals.

**Effects:**
- Strength, Dexterity, Constitution: increased
- Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma: decreased

Known cases in the party's orbit: **[[Edgar Bonnybluff]]**, observed by [[Rene]] in [[Arinthal Harbor]].

## The Order's Response

The [[Order of Bone]] treats magic as categorically eradicable. Its hunters track magic users, the Manawracked, and by extension anyone who shelters them. The Order produces [[Thramnium]], a metal with limited magical corruption resistance.

## Uncontrolled Magic — Encountered Cases

- **The Moons' Threnody**: A research vessel studying magic at sea. An artifact the captain recovered infected the crew with a compulsion to kill each other. One survivor: [[Corren Saithe]].
- **The metallic orb from the Threnody**: When [[Rene]] touched it, it opened. The interior contained a water sphere holding a figure made of stars. The entity disappeared upon reaching sunlight.

## The Manamade

The **[[Manamade]]** are a loose collection of disparate groups united only by a desire not to be killed by the rest of the world for touching magic. They are not an organization with leadership or coordination. They are people who have decided not to die quietly.
