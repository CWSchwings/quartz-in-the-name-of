# The World

**Name:** Thauviathun  
**Moons:** Three

---

## The Two Worlds

The world of Thauviathun is divided between two major continents:

**[[Arctoriam]]** — the Old World. Where the party is from. Known nations include [[Zhelesyr]], [[Tir Zafiri]], [[Vallume]], [[Calvatharn]], [[Myrenthal]], [[Kaelmaris]], [[Darathia]], and [[Durathia]].

**[[Zylandrom]]** — the New World. Dinosaurs. Indigenous species. As yet largely unmapped by the nations of [[Arctoriam]], though expeditions are ongoing.

## Calendar

- 1 year = 13 months
- 1 month = 4 weeks
- 1 week = 7 days
- 1 day = 24 hours
