# Species of the World

The world is home to two broad groupings of sapient peoples, separated by geography and deep history.

---

## Arctoriam Peoples

Native to the Old World continent of [[Arctoriam]].

### Humanoids
The most widespread people of Arctoriam. Maximum lifespan approximately 90 years. Includes the humans, elves, dwarves, halflings, and related kindred who populate the nations of the Old World.

### Goblinoids
Also native to Arctoriam. Maximum lifespan approximately 45 years. Includes orcs, goblins, and related peoples.

---

## Zylandrom Peoples

Native to [[Zylandrom]], the New World. The Zylandrom peoples were encountered when explorers from Arctoriam crossed to the new continent.

### Reptilefolk
The longest-lived of the Zylandrom peoples. Maximum lifespan approximately 150 years. Includes lizardfolk and related lineages.

### Beastfolk
Warm-blooded, mammalian peoples of Zylandrom. Maximum lifespan approximately 70 years. Includes tabaxi, harengon, tortle, and related kindred.

### Amphibianfolk
Amphibious peoples native to Zylandrom. Maximum lifespan approximately 70 years.

- **Bullywug** — a common phenotype of Amphibianfolk

### Avianfolk
Avian peoples of Zylandrom. The shortest-lived of the New World species. Maximum lifespan approximately 40 years. Includes ravenfolk and related kindred.
