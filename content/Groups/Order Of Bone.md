The Order of Bone, often simply referred to as "The Order" in [[Arctoriam]] due to its ubiquity across national and cultural lines, is a widespread Magic hunting organization dedicated to the end of all magic in the world.

Magic is a disease, and the Order is the Cure.

The Order of Bone is not directly tied to any government, though many governments fund local chapters of the order.

Externally the Order is referred to as a single monolithic entity but internally, the Order is comprised of several sub-orders:
