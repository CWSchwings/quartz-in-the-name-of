# Serath Vornea

**Affiliation:** [[Order of Bone]]  
**Role:** Head of the Order of Bone

---

Serath Vornea leads the [[Order of Bone]] at the organizational level. This makes him distinct from **[[Jareth Varcryn]]**, who heads the Order's chapter in [[Zhelesyr]].

The Order of Bone operates across national lines and maintains multiple sub-orders. Serath Vornea's position sits at the top of that structure.
