# Dalisay De Lenti

**Affiliation:** [[De Lenti Family]], [[Ember Alliance]]  
**Status:** Deceased

---

Dalisay De Lenti was one of [[Bennett]]'s parents and a member of the [[Ember Alliance]]. She died during the flight from [[Zhelesyr]] when the Alliance was betrayed to the [[Thalorian Dynasty]].
