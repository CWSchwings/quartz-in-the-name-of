# Calvados

**Location:** [[Salty Gull]], [[Arinthal Harbor]]

---

Calvados works at the **[[Salty Gull]]**, a dive bar in [[Arinthal Harbor]]. He is a contact of [[Johanna]]'s — one of several connections she maintains in the city through her prior work with the [[Tidebreakers]].
