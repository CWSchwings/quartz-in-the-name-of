# Bennett

**Full name:** Benedicta De Lenti  
**Species:** Human  
**Class:** Gunslinger  
**Affiliation:** [[Captains]], formerly merchant ship bodyguard  
**Role aboard ship:** Gunfighter, ship's conscience

---

## Who She Is

Bennett was born on a ship. Her parents were among the founding members of the **[[Ember Alliance]]**, an uprising against Crown Prince **[[Vaerik Draevenn]]** and the **[[Thalorian Dynasty]]** in [[Zhelesyr]]. When her parents learned they were expecting, they made the decision to abandon the fight. That decision cost lives. Other members of the Alliance called it a betrayal.

Their escape was betrayed to the Thalorian crown. They fled in the night. Some of her parents died ensuring the others and Bennett survived. Those deaths ultimately sparked further rebellions — the irony being that her parents' withdrawal from the fight lit more fires than their continued presence would have.

She grew up on ships, her surviving parents working crew wherever they could find berths, running from the Thalorian reach. By the time the family settled in [[Zhelesyr]] and opened a smithy, Bennett was already shaped by their stories of sacrifice and loss.

Her eyes shine like a cat's in the dark. Her blood has a pearlescent sheen. Her parents say she was blessed by the act of their sacrifice. She hasn't decided what to make of that.

## Family

Her living parents:
- **[[Faustus De Lenti]]** — orc, smith, gunsmith
- **[[Mayari De Lenti]]** — elf, purple hair, fighter
- **[[Inka De Lenti]]** — human, nonbinary, scribe; alive but recovering from injury

Three parents are deceased. Their names were Benicio, Luca, and Dalisay.

Her childhood friend: **[[Bastian Malivorna]]**, a nobleman of [[Durathia]] and a person she trusts more than most.

## Character

Principled. She named [[Caspian]]'s naval service as slavery when he was still treating it as something else. She manages the ship's relationships — between crew, between captains — with a practical care that doesn't ask for recognition.

She carries the weight of a revolution she didn't choose and can't fully put down. She's making her own sense of it on the way.

## The [[Order of Bone]]

The Order caught her early. She escaped by luck. She hasn't forgotten.
