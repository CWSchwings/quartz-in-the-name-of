Benedicta, fondly known as Bennett by her parents, is the only child of the [[De Lentis]]. Her parents were the founding leaders of an uprising against [[Corwin Thalorian]]’s unjust rule.   
After years of fighting, they found they were pregnant and soon after made the difficult decision to abandon the fight. This sparked outrage and controversy within the movement. They called themselves the [[Ember Alliance]].  
They fled in the dead of night. However, their movements were betrayed to [[Corwin Thalorian]] and they were ambushed. Some of her parents paid with their lives to ensure that the others and Bennett would escape and live. This act of martyrdom ultimately sparked more rebellions against the [[Thalorian Dynasty]], even though her parents' own rebellion was quashed.  
Bennett was born on the ship her surviving parents used to escape. As she grew, it slowly became apparent that Bennett was blessed by the act of her parent’s sacrifice, or at least that is what her living parents told her. Her eyes shone like a cat’s in the dark, and her blood had a pearlescent sheen to it.  
Bennett’s childhood was spent on many ships her parents worked on, and they continued to run from the reach of the [[Thalorian Dynasty]]. She was shaped by her parent’s heroism, sacrifice and grief. In her teenage years, the remains of the family settled in [[Zhelesyr]], opening their own smithy to support themselves.  
Now grown, she carries the legacy of a revolution she never chose—haunted by the lives lost, driven to make sense of their sacrifice and find her own purpose. She decided it was time for her to see the world and gain experience as an individual away from her protective parents.
