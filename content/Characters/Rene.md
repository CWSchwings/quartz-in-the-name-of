# Rene

**Species:** Human  
**Class:** Ranger  
**Affiliation:** [[Captains]], formerly [[Order of Bone]]  
**Role aboard ship:** Lookout, ranger, reluctant advisor on magic

---

## Who He Is

Rene was a member of the [[Order of Bone]] for over a decade. He joined as a teenager, after years of reading the Order's correspondence as a courier and deciding he wanted that life — the travel, the danger, the purpose.

He was trained to fight by **[[Evaine]]**, who was his partner and later his wife.

He believed, for a time, that hunting magic users was a necessary evil. He still doesn't dispute the danger of uncontrolled magic. He stopped being able to live with the kills.

At some point during his service, he stopped killing the people he was sent to find and started helping them escape instead. He found a ship captain who moved people to relative safety. For years he would locate a target, hide them, arrange the route out, and report back to the Order that they were dead. He got away with it longer than anyone should have.

**[[Evaine]]** found out. She followed him to the port, to the smuggler's ship. When he admitted to the years of deception, she set the ship on fire and tried to kill everyone aboard. He ran. He got on the nearest ship going somewhere else.

That ship was caught in a three-way naval engagement in a storm. A sea monster finished what the storm started. He washed up on a dinosaur island with strangers.

## Character

Guarded. Cagey about magic in ways that read as paranoid until you understand his history. His first instinct on encountering the *[[Moons' Threnody]]* was to sink it and sail away. He was right. He then touched the magical artifact anyway, which is the kind of contradiction that defines him.

He cared, even when caring made him a traitor. That empathy has not left him, though he doesn't wear it comfortably. He gave Vinnie a rapier and named it FirstBlood because he remembered what his own first kill felt like and wanted to say something true about it.

## Known Secrets

- Former [[Order of Bone]] member, revealed in [[Zhelesyr]] during the prison break.
- [[Evaine]] is still alive and presumably still a skilled hunter who knows exactly what he looks like.
