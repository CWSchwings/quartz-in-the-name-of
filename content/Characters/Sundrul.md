# Sundrul

**Species:** Orc  
**Affiliation:** [[Tidebreakers]]  
**Location:** [[Arinthal Harbor]], [[Tir Zafiri]]

---

Sundrul is an orc man with a practical relationship to coin and a wariness of the [[Tir Zafiri Navy]]'s expanding reach. He leads the [[Tidebreakers]] — a protection racket and smugglers' guild operating out of [[Arinthal Harbor]].

He has a soft spot for [[Johanna]], who worked for the Tidebreakers for a time to buy protection for her family.

When the party arrived at [[Arinthal Harbor]] in [[Session 08 - Incognito Mode|Session 08]], Sundrul offered information about the mercenary expedition to the New World in exchange for a favor: recovering a magical artifact from the local jail before the [[Order of Bone]] could collect it. The party would be permitted to keep any other artifacts found inside.
