# B'Rieph and Bauble

**Relation:** [[Bishop]]'s closest friends; presumed dead by him

---

## B'Rieph

[[Bishop]]'s best friend. Himbo-coded. Part of a romantic throuple with Bishop and [[Bauble]]. Escaped [[Bishop]]'s tribe alongside Bishop and Bauble when Bishop was exiled. Presumed dead by Bishop after an imperialist raid on a village they had settled into. **Not dead.**

## Bauble

Full name: p'Lish. [[Bishop]]'s long-time crush — and, unknown to him, the mother of his children. Deaf; Bishop and B'Rieph both learned sign language for her. Kind, stern, and capable in a fight. Presumed dead by Bishop after the raid. **Not dead.**

She and B'Rieph managed to escape with most of her clutch of eggs. They are raising three baby bullywugs together. They mourn Bishop deeply, believing him dead.

---

## The Situation

It has been eight years since Bishop was separated from them. He believes he lost them to an imperialist raid. He has not stopped looking for them, in the way that people look for things they expect never to find.

He has three children he does not know exist.
