# Elias Braith

**Status:** Alive; location: a tropical port somewhere  
**Former affiliation:** [[Tir Zafiri Navy]]

---

An old sailor who used to drink in the rundown taverns of [[Arinthal Harbor]] and tell stories about the ocean and the things beyond the map's edge. Most people dismissed him as a drunkard and a liar.

[[Caspian]] believed him. Those stories became Caspian's plan for his life.

Elias had a falling-out with the [[Tir Zafiri Navy]] and became a privateer. He is holed up in some tropical port. He was a bad influence. He was, by Caspian's accounting, the most interesting person in his childhood.
