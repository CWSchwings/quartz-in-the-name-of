# Caspian

**Full name:** Caspian O'Leary  
**Species:** Human (partial siren descent — unknown to him)  
**Class:** Pugilist  
**Affiliation:** [[Captains]], formerly [[Tir Zafiri Navy]]  
**Role aboard ship:** Helmsman, close-quarters fighter

---

## Who He Is

Caspian is twenty-two years old. Six and a half feet of sun-tanned, cauliflowered, mildly green-tinned sailor. He grew up in [[Tir Zafiri]] beside his father **[[Edward O'Leary]]**, who washed ashore on a driftwood raft with a baby Caspian two years after his merchant ship was attacked and sunk. Edward claimed sirens kept him alive as a plaything. Everyone assumed he was mad. He referred to Caspian as "my little albatross." He was mostly drunk or in debt.

An old sailor named **[[Elias Braith]]**, formerly of the [[Tir Zafiri Navy]] turned privateer, told Caspian stories about the ocean. Those stories became the plan. When the time came, Caspian joined the navy rather than wait for them to force him.

He has pointed ears. Green-tinted skin. If he's away from the ocean for extended periods, his skin begins to scale in places. He has not asked about this.

His mother is **[[Auriel Crestwave]]**. She is a siren. He does not know this.

## Character

Act first, ask questions never. He declared the pirates under arrest on the beach of a dinosaur island. He tried to wrestle a plesiosaur. He does not hesitate.

He takes authority seriously — gave it, expected it, trusted it. The conversation with [[Bennett]] about what his naval service actually was landed hard. The first mate and captain had been skimming his wages into a "savings program" he never agreed to. He had been told it was for his benefit. He had believed it. He is working out what that means.

## What He Doesn't Know

- His mother is a siren.
- The sea may have a claim on him that goes beyond his own choices.
- His minor physical anomalies are not coincidental.
