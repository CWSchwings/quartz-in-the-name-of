# Zara Firebeard

**Species:** Human  
**Origin:** [[Tir Zafiri]]  
**Ship:** *[[Celestial Shield]]*  
**Affiliation:** [[Emerald Expansion]]

---

Zara Firebeard is a corpulent woman with flame-red hair, one of the captains of the [[Emerald Expansion]]. She met the party at the Restful Skies inn in [[Khareem]], questioned the crew about their experience, and was given a vague account.

She is a capable captain and, based on reputation, not someone to underestimate.
