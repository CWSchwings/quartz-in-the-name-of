# Jareth Varcryn

**Affiliation:** [[Order of Bone]]  
**Role:** Head of the [[Zhelesyr]] chapter

---

Jareth Varcryn runs the [[Order of Bone]]'s operations in [[Zhelesyr]]. He coordinates with Governor Elrik Thelorian and Captain of the Guard Thalor Duskbane to round up suspected magic users and those associated with them — tieflings, bullywugs, lizardfolk.

The party broke out his prisoners and fled under cannon fire. Varcryn knows they exist. The forged Order credentials the party used during the prison break were made to appear signed under his authority.
