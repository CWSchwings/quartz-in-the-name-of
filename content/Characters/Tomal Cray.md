# Tomal Cray

**Species:** Human  
**Role:** Linguist  
**Affiliation:** Crew of the [[Bleeding Heart]] / [[Take Heart]]

---

Tomal Cray funded a voyage to the New World to establish a trading outpost. His hired ship, *[[The Graven Deep]]*, put him over the side for being insufferable company and marooned him on a small island.

The party picked him up during [[Session 04 - What's Magics Precious|Session 04]] and kept him aboard. He speaks approximately fifteen languages, which makes him useful enough to excuse the rest.

When Merrow blocked the Edgar Bonnybluff salvage site, Tomal identified their language as Seravaxan and [[Rene]] took over the negotiation.
