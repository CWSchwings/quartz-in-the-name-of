# Thalia Stormrider

**Affiliation:** Constellate Archer  
**Ship:** *[[Moons' Threnody]]*  
**Role:** Captain

---

Thalia Stormrider was the captain of the *[[Moons' Threnody]]*, a research vessel dedicated to the study of magic. Her crew chose to conduct their experiments far from shore in order to minimize the risk to populated areas. The precaution proved insufficient. Their research eventually turned catastrophically against the crew, and the ship's company killed one another. **[[Corren Saithe]]** was the only survivor.

What exactly Thalia's fate was — and what she knew or attempted before the end — has not been established.
