# Johanna

**Full name:** Johanna Bonnybluff  
**Species:** Halfling  
**Class:** Rogue  
**Affiliation:** [[Captains]], formerly [[Tir Zafiri Navy]], formerly pirate  
**Role aboard ship:** Stealth, negotiation, local contacts

---

## Who She Is

Johanna grew up in [[Tir Zafiri]] as one of many children taken in by her adoptive mother **[[Mathilde Bonnybluff]]** — a no-nonsense woman who ran a fish market, hauled nets, washed clothes, and somehow kept the family together. Her adoptive father **[[Alwyn Bonnybluff]]** left when Johanna was very young. She was told he left to travel. The truth is that Mathilde sent him away because his magic practice put the family at risk.

She has too many siblings to name conveniently. Her brother **[[Edgar Bonnybluff]]** is a captain in the [[Tir Zafiri Navy]]. He arranged for her to serve in the navy rather than rot in a cell. She didn't love the deal. She took it.

She worked for the **[[Tidebreakers]]** before the navy — a protection racket and smuggler's guild in [[Arinthal Harbor]]. **[[Sundrul]]**, their leader, has a soft spot for her. She used the connection to buy protection for her family.

Her ex-captain was **[[Isabel Coronado Du Zof]]**. Her ex-first mate was **[[Sloane]]** — a man whose savings program for crew wages was, in [[Bennett]]'s plain terms, theft from people without the power to refuse. Johanna already knew this about Sloane. It took [[Caspian]] longer to see it.

## The Bracelet

Johanna came into possession of a bracelet with properties she did not fully understand. When she used it to observe a woman named **Sotha** who wore an identical one, she and Sotha switched locations briefly. She caught a glimpse of [[Pot]] and Vinnie before coming back.

Examination afterward: longer hair at her wrist where the bracelet sat. She set it aside.

She told [[Caspian]] in a quiet conversation that magic and the wolf transformation both represent a kind of bodily power she doesn't usually feel she has. The appeal is real, even knowing the risks.

## The Tidebreakers Arrangement

Johanna has arranged for the keep she controls to be used as a base by a criminal organization operating in her name. **Shadowfin** manages it in her absence and maintains communications. This is an ongoing obligation.
