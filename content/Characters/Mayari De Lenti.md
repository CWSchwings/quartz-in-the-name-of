# Mayari De Lenti

**Species:** Human (noted as having purple hair)  
**Gender:** Female  
**Occupation:** Fighter  
**Affiliation:** [[De Lenti Family]]  
**Location:** [[Draekara]], [[Zhelesyr]]

---

Mayari De Lenti is one of [[Bennett]]'s living parents. She has purple hair and was a fighter — one of the surviving members of the group that escaped [[Zhelesyr]] after the [[Ember Alliance]] was betrayed.

She is alive and settled with [[Faustus De Lenti]] and [[Inka De Lenti]] in [[Draekara]].
