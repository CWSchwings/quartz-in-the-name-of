# Elara Shadowstride

**Affiliation:** Unknown; operates independently  
**Ship:** Unnamed black vessel

---

First encountered attacking a [[Calvatharn]] ship near the [[Myrenthal]] coast. [[Bennett]] made contact and introduced the *[[Bleeding Heart]]* as a ship run by committee. Elara traded for 100 gold profit and parted friendly.

She suggested the crew come find her once they had proven themselves in real combat. She has not been encountered since, but the offer stands.
