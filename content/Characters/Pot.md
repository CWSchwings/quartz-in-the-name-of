# Pot

**Full name:** Push and Pull of the Ocean Tide  
**Species:** Tabaxi  
**Class:** Monk  
**Affiliation:** [[Captains]], formerly pirate crew  
**Role aboard ship:** Crow's nest watch

---

## Who He Is

Pot has spent his entire life at sea. The ship was his world before any island or port had a claim on him. He occupied the crow's nest — the highest point, the first to see anything coming, the last place anyone bothers you — and that suited him.

When the sea monster wrecked three ships in a storm, Pot went into the water and ended up on the island with the rest. His first act there of any note was trying to free a herbivore dinosaur from a pit trap and getting hit by its tail. He freed it anyway.

## Character

Pot acts in flashes — quick movement, quick decision, occasional stumble. He's not reckless exactly, but he has a short gap between observation and motion. He's also the one who swam out alone to the [[Calvatharn]] ship and captured the scientist Thebrin Quass, which required both speed and the willingness to get there first.

He watches things carefully from above. He talks to people quietly. He thinks about the crew.

## Present Concerns

**Silver.** After the wolf fight at [[Bastian Malivorna]]'s estate, Pot burned himself on a silver coin in his own pocket. He went to [[Corren Saithe]] first, who sent him to [[Thebrin Quass]]. She reviewed the options. Amputation of the affected area. Aconite. Neither appealing.

He has not told everyone. He told [[Bennett]]. He discussed keeping a schedule around feeding SK — a creature the crew maintains — and noted that Vinnie's handling of it is conspicuous.
