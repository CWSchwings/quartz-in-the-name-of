# Thebrin Quass

**Species:** Elf  
**Affiliation:** Formerly [[Calvatharn]], currently crew of the [[Bleeding Heart]]  
**Role:** Scientist, naturalist

---

## History

Thebrin Quass was the scientist aboard the [[Calvatharn]] research vessel anchored off the [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]]. When the party took the ship, she was the only person aboard — she had not gone ashore. [[Pot]] apprehended her. [[Rene]] wanted her killed. Most of the party disagreed.

She stayed.

## Aboard the Ship

She is useful. She has expertise in natural science, [[Calvatharn]] technical knowledge, and access to information about a nation the rest of the crew actively stole from. She calls [[Pot]] *Push* — an apparently sincere rendering of his name that she finds satisfying.

When [[Pot]] came to her with concerns about possible lycanthropy after the wolf fight, she gave him the honest assessment: no clean cure exists. Best options are amputation or aconite. She did not soften it.
