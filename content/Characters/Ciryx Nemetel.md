# Ciryx Nemetel

**Affiliation:** [[Calvatharn]]  
**Role:** Director of Mobile Extraction Operations

---

Ciryx Nemetel holds the title of Director of Mobile Extraction Operations for [[Calvatharn]]. He is established as [[Bishop]]'s nemesis — what that history entails exactly has not been fully disclosed.

[[Calvatharn]] is a technocratic slaveowner state. Its extraction operations — mobile or otherwise — are conducted with that logic underpinning them.
