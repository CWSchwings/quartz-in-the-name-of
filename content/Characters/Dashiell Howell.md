# Dashiell Howell

**Affiliation:** [[Tir Zafiri]] government  
**Position:** High-ranking official

---

Dashiell Howell is a high-ranking [[Tir Zafiri]] official who opposes the [[Calvatharn]] alliance. The **Mandic family** led the coalition that voted to join [[Calvatharn]]; Howell dissented.

He was contacted by the party upon their return to [[Arinthal Harbor]] in [[Session 40 - 42 - Back to Tir Zafiri|Session 40]].

A potential ally in navigating the changed political landscape.
