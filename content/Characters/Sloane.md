First Mate of the [[Zns Thalassir]].  
"The Shark"  
A greasy mound of sea scum zipped inside the ill-fitting resemblance of a man. [[Sloane]] is the true captain of the [[Zns Thalassir]] and the reason many of her crew are aboard in the first place. He is renowned for hanging about the docks and preying on drunkards, dullards, and the down-trodden at the gambling tables and forcing them onto the ship to work off their debts. Sloane starts a "savings" program for each new recruit where he skims some of their wages off the top because "You can’t be trusted to save it, so I’ll do it for you." The common sailors despise Sloane with a passion but he’s ensured compliance by hiring his own lackeys to all the ship’s ranked positions.
