# Sloane

**Alias:** "The Shark"  
**Affiliation:** Formerly *[[Zns Thalassir]]*  
**Role:** First Mate

---

Sloane is the actual power aboard the *Zns Thalassir*, with Captain [[Isabel Coronado Du Zof]] providing the rank while he provides the control. Most of the crew serve because Sloane recruited them — preying on drunkards, debtors, and the down-and-out at harbor gambling tables and forcing them to work off what they owe.

He runs a "savings program" for new recruits: he skims wages off the top on their behalf, because in his words, "you can't be trusted to save it." His lackeys hold every ranked position on the ship to ensure compliance.

[[Bennett]] named it plainly when [[Johanna]] and [[Caspian]] described how they came to serve: that was slavery.
