# Thebrin's List

*Provided to Vinnie in [[Session 40 - 42 - Back to Tir Zafiri|Session 40]]. These are [[Thebrin Quass]]'s personal and professional grievances against [[Calvatharn]] scientists. This list may become actionable.*

---

## The Scientists

**Svetlana Bouchard** — idiopsychology  
Exhibits abnormal physical capabilities. Interesting as a research subject; ideally taken alive. Last seen in [[Seravax]].

**Nikita Mencher** — thermokinematics  
Thebrin's ex-lover. Purely spite. Left Thebrin for another after using her to secure a research position. Currently in the New World.

**Mina McDowell** — oneirology  
Described by Thebrin as worthless. Continually secures funding despite no published research. Rarely leaves her lab in the [[Calvatharn]] capital.

**Florinda Worknesh** — metallurgy  
An essential part of the [[Calvatharn]] war effort. Rarely leaves her lab in the capital.

**Brynhildr Nyman** — epidemiology  
Dangerous and unprincipled. Responsible for thousands of deaths and a large portion of [[Calvatharn]]'s biological weapons program.

**Ebenezer Vartanian** — cryptology  
Personal. Attacked Thebrin's reputation over shared grant funding. Enjoys espionage, torture, and languages.

**Godric Miltiadis** — pseudoptics  
Runs a shipbuilding program on [[Calvatharn]]'s southern edge. His research may be useful to the *[[Take Heart]]*.
