# Vaerik Draevenn

**Affiliation:** [[Thalorian Dynasty]], [[Zhelesyr]]  
**Role:** Crown Prince of Zhelesyr

---

Vaerik Draevenn is the Crown Prince of [[Zhelesyr]] and a personal nemesis of the [[Ember Alliance]] — and by extension, of [[Bennett]] and her family. The **[[De Lenti]]** family's flight from [[Zhelesyr]] was a direct consequence of the Alliance's conflict with his rule.

The Thalorian crown's pursuit of Alliance members led to the deaths of several of Bennett's parents. The survivors fled at night. Some of those they left behind did not survive the night either.
