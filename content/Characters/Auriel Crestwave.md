# Auriel Crestwave

**Species:** Siren  
**Relation:** [[Caspian]]'s mother

---

Auriel Crestwave is a siren. She is [[Caspian]]'s mother.

[[Caspian]] does not know this. He was told his mother "was with the sea" and assumed she drowned. His father [[Edward O'Leary]] claimed sirens attacked his ship and kept him alive as a plaything — everyone dismissed him as mad. He was telling the truth.

Caspian's pointed ears, green-tinted skin, and the scaling that appears when he's too long away from the ocean are her inheritance.

What Auriel knows of her son, and whether she has any interest in him, is unknown.
