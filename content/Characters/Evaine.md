# Evaine

**Species:** Human  
**Affiliation:** [[Order of Bone]]  
**Role:** Hunter

---

Evaine is a skilled hunter for the [[Order of Bone]] who takes genuine satisfaction in killing those touched by magic. She trained [[Rene]] to fight. She was his partner and later his wife.

She discovered his betrayal — that for years he had been finding magic users, hiding them, arranging their escape, and reporting false deaths back to the Order — when she followed him to a port and found him on a smuggler's ship with the people he was supposed to have killed.

When he admitted to it, she set the ship on fire. She tried to kill everyone on board. [[Rene]] was outmatched and ran. He found the first ship going anywhere else.

She is alive. She is still a hunter. She knows what [[Rene]] looks like.
