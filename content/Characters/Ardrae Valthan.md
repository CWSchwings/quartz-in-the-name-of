# Ardrae Valthan

**Species:** Human  
**Gender:** Female  
**Affiliation:** [[Vallume]]  
**Role:** Queen of Vallume

---

Ardrae Valthan is the Queen of [[Vallume]]. Her nation borders [[Kaelmaris]] on the ocean and is currently allied with [[Darathia]] in opposition to the [[Calvatharn]]-[[Tir Zafiri]] alliance.
