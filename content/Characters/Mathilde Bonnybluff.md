# Mathilde Bonnybluff

**Species:** Dwarf  
**Location:** [[Arinthal Harbor]], [[Tir Zafiri]]  
**Relation:** [[Johanna]]'s adoptive mother

---

Mathilde Bonnybluff is a no-nonsense woman who raised multiple children largely alone after her husband [[Alwyn Bonnybluff]] was sent away. She worked the fish market, hauled nets, washed clothes, and haggled hard. Rough hands, practical instincts.

She almost always has one or two young children about — she takes in strays. That is her weakness, though she would not call it one.

She told [[Johanna]] that Alwyn left to travel. The truth she kept: she sent him away because his practice of magic put the family at risk.
