[[Johanna]]'s adoptive Mother  
[[Female]] [[Dwarf]]  
Nice, but not kind.  
Had to raise several children on her own after her husband [[Alwyn Bonnybluff]] departed for parts unknown.  
A no-nonsense woman, she’s not one for baking or cooking, but is more used to rough hands from washing clothes, hauling fish nets and haggling sales at the fish market.  
Almost always has one or two young children there.  
Young children are her weak spot though she can’t really afford to house and feed them all
