# Alwyn Bonnybluff

**Relation:** [[Johanna]]'s adoptive father

---

Alwyn left when [[Johanna]] was very young. [[Mathilde Bonnybluff]] told the children he left to travel. She did not tell them the truth: she sent him away, because his practice of magic was too dangerous to the family.

He may or may not be alive. [[Johanna]] has no reason to think kindly of him, regardless — to her, he is the man who walked out and left them all to manage without him.

Whether he knows his departure was involuntary is unknown.
