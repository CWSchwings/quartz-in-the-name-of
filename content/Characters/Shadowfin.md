# Shadowfin

**Affiliation:** Independent smuggler; associate of the [[Captains]]  
**Alias used by others:** "The Courier"

---

Shadowfin is a smuggler with reach into multiple ports. [[Rene]] learned of him through [[Edgar Bonnybluff]] during the [[Khareem]] stop.

He has since become a fixed point for the party's operations. Roles taken on:

- Left at the keep in [[Durathia]] to manage criminal contacts on [[Johanna]]'s behalf
- Maintains communications between the keep and the criminal network
- Has instructions to contact **Aslan** at the **Stuffy Scabbard** in [[Vallumos]] if the party needs to reach him

[[Rene]] asked him to sell a shield and a mace. He did.
