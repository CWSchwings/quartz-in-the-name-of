# Mirath the Remembered

**Type:** Oblex  
**First encountered:** [[Vallumos]] Sewers

---

An Oblex — a creature that absorbs and stores the memories of its victims. First encountered in the sewers beneath [[Vallumos]]. It attacked, absorbed memories from multiple [[Captains]], and fled. It returned for more on at least one subsequent occasion.

## Memories Absorbed

At last count:
- [[Pot]]: 4 memories
- [[Caspian]]: 4 memories
- [[Bennett]]: 4+ memories

The Oblex holds these memories as part of itself. What it does with them, and whether it has begun using them, is unknown.
