# Therium Vale

**Gender:** Female  
**Occupation:** Pirate Captain  
**Status:** Mostly legendary — extent of her current activity unclear

---

Therium Vale is a pirate captain known more by reputation than by direct encounter. She wears a coat made from patchwork white silks — each panel taken from the sail of a ship she has sunk. The coat is a record of her career. The list is long.

Whether she is still active and where she operates are questions that do not have confirmed answers.
