# Varla Elarion

**Role:** Healer / Medic  
**Affiliation:** Crew of the [[Take Heart]]

---

[[Bennett]] recruited Varla Elarion personally during the mass recruitment drive in [[Myrenthal]]. 

When the *Take Heart* encountered sweet-tasting seawater laced with parasites, Varla and [[Thebrin Quass]] worked together to develop a cure before the crew sickened badly.

Varla is owed 10 gold.
