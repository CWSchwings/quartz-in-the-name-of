[[Caspian]]'s father.  
Part of a merchant sailing vessel that was lost at sea.  
Two years after the ship sank, washed ashore in [[Tir Zafiri]] with a driftwood raft and a baby [[Caspian]]. He claimed he was kept alive by sirens that attacked his crew, and was assumed to be insane.  
[[Caspian]] grew up beside the absent-minded husk of Edward, often drunk or in debt. Edward referred to [[Caspian]] as "my little albatross."

Private Part (GM)

In reality they were beset by sirens and majority of the crew drowned or were eaten. Edward was kept alive as a plaything for a time.
