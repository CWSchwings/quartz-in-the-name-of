# Edward O'Leary

**Relation:** [[Caspian]]'s father

---

Edward O'Leary was on a merchant sailing vessel that was lost at sea. Two years after the ship sank, he washed ashore in [[Tir Zafiri]] on a driftwood raft with an infant [[Caspian]].

He claimed sirens attacked his crew — that most drowned or were eaten, and that he was kept alive as a plaything for a time. Everyone assumed he was mad. He was not.

He grew up calling Caspian "my little albatross." He spent most of Caspian's childhood drunk and in debt. He was not present in any meaningful sense.

**[[Auriel Crestwave]]** is the siren who kept him. She is Caspian's mother. Edward knows this. He has never explained it in terms Caspian was willing to hear.
