# Isabel Coronado Du Zof

**Affiliation:** [[Coronado Family]], formerly captain of the *[[Zns Thalassir]]*  
**Origin:** [[Tir Zafiri]]

---

Isabel is the fifth child of the [[Coronado Family]] — a noble house that acquires its wealth through mining rights. With no innate talents and no path to becoming family head, she sought to build her own name through naval command.

Her ambition is to whip the [[Tir Zafiri Navy]] into something capable of competing with [[Zhelesyr]] and [[Vallume]]. She pursues any task that makes her look good. She is ruthlessly self-serving in command.

She was [[Johanna]]'s captain. She was [[Caspian]]'s captain. The actual running of the ship was [[Sloane]]'s domain; Isabel supplied the authority.
