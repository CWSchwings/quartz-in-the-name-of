Captain of the [[Zns Thalassir]]  
Fifth child of the [[Coronado Family]] with no innate talents or hope of becoming the family head.  
Seeks to develop a name for herself to win gold, honors, and the respect of her peers. Thinks the best way to do so is to whip the [[Tir Zafiri Navy]] into shape, capable of competing on the same level as [[Zhelesyr]] and [[Vallume]]. A ruthlessly selfish captain taking on any task to make herself look good.
