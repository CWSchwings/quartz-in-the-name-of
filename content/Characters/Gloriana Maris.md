# Gloriana Maris

**Role:** Matron of [[Seasong Haven]]  
**Location:** [[Arinthal Harbor]], [[Tir Zafiri]]

---

Gloriana Maris runs the **[[Seasong Haven]]** orphanage in [[Arinthal Harbor]], funded by noble patronage. She tried to keep a young [[Caspian]] fed and in lessons when he showed up. He was never one for sitting still. She did what she could.
