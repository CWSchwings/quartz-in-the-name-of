Private Part (GM)

Full Name: p'Neep-rüdab  
Age: Full Grown (whatever the equivalent of a 30 human years is in Bullywug years)  
Connections:  
[[B'Rieph]]  
[[Bauble]]

Personality:  
Observant, calculating, a bit impulsive  
Cheeky & sarcastic when he does talk. A little silly.  
Got a bit of a sailor's mouth iykwim  
Strong hyperfixation on projects - will prioritize these over everything else when it comes down to the wire  
Loves to solve a problem.  
Loves to drink.  
In this pirate business because he's anti-imperialist and wants to fuck shit up. Also, the deaths of his best friends and loves of his life haunts him while he's on land. He's constantly looking for them and becomes restless when they're docked.

Background:  
Basing [[Bullywug]] society loosely off [this](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Bullywug#google_vignette) forgotten realms wiki page. I'm imagining his tribe to be "slightly" less brutal but still carrying the big thematic elements.

Was a quiet but mischievous youth, claiming he was going to conquer the untamed wilds with his inventions and getting beat up for it. (But also getting revenge on the bullys with traps and stuff)

Learned how to use a bunch of artisan's tools on his own and managed to impress his tribe with new gizmos and killing tools so he was largely left alone even though he didn't have the same physical prowess as the other males in his tribe.

Somehow he pisses the tribe leader off, he has to fight and then he loses his tongue and gets kicked out and left for dead. His best friend [[B'Rieph]] & other best friend/crush [[Bauble]] decide to run away together. They successfully escape but are then caught up in an imperialist raid(?) of a village they'd settled into. Bishop presumes [[Bauble]] & [[B'Rieph]] are dead as he spends 3 years in forced servitude on an imperial ship (or something similar. I want him to have a healthy dose of anger towards the establishment so as to fuel the fire of being a pirate) before his ship is attacked by pirates and he's picked up to be a deckhand on the pirate ship.

When we start the campaign it's been a total of 8 years since he was separated from [[Bauble]]& [[B'Rieph]].

Character Growth/Story Points:

SURPRISE! His best friends are not dead and have in fact been mostly okay.

DOUBLE SURPRISE! [[B'Rieph]] and [[Bauble]] managed to escape with most of [[Bauble]]'s clutch of eggs and are now raising 3 baby [[Bullywug]]s together. They deeply mourn the death of Bishop and that he'll never get to see his kids grow up.
