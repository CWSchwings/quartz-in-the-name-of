# Petra Bonnybluff

**Relation:** [[Johanna]]'s younger sister  
**Affiliation:** [[Tidebreakers]]

---

Petra is following in [[Johanna]]'s footsteps — she is part of the [[Tidebreakers]] and appears to be heading for a life on the sea. Small, blonde, curly short hair, slender build.

She has taken a secret interest in magic that is also driving her toward the open ocean.

[[Rene]] and [[Bennett]] went to speak with her during [[Session 30 - Durathia|Sessions 30]] and again in [[Session 40 - 42 - Back to Tir Zafiri|Session 42]].
