[[Ranger]] [[Human]] [[Soldier]] [[Chaotic]] [[Good]]

Private Part (GM)

[[Rene]] first encountered the [[Order of Bone]] when he was a kid. Back then, he worked as a courier of sorts. His job was to train messenger birds that delivered correspondence from his home city, a service the local [[Order Of Bone]] chapter often co-opted for its own use. Thanks to a casual disregard for confidentiality, he grew up aspiring to what he read of them – the travels, danger, coin, and purpose.

[[Rene]] joined the [[Order Of Bone]] as a teenager. He was trained to fight by his girlfriend and future wife, [[Evaine]]. While [[Rene]] initially believed their work was a necessary evil serving the greater good, his own resolve proved more susceptible to guilt with time.

At some point over his decade of service, [[Rene]]stopped killing the people he was sent to hunt and started helping them instead. In his travels, he met a ship captain from the new world who was willing to help smuggle magic users to relative safety. Rayner would find them, hide them, arrange their escape, then inform the Order of their death. He got away with it for longer than expected until, inevitably, the lies caught up to him.

[[Evaine]] discovered his betrayal first. She followed him on his next hunt and confronted him at port on the smuggler’s ship. When he admitted to going behind her back for years, she set fire to the ship and tried to kill everyone on board, especially him. He was outmatched and forced to run. Hoping to evade her pursuit on the open ocean, he got aboard the next ship he saw going anywhere else as fast as possible.
