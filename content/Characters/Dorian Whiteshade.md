# Dorian Whiteshade

**Origin:** [[Zhelesyr]]  
**Ship:** *[[Silvered Edge]]*  
**Affiliation:** [[Emerald Expansion]]

---

A captain operating out of [[Zhelesyr]], captaining the *Silvered Edge*. [[Pot]] spotted him examining the *[[Bleeding Heart]]* up close in [[Khareem]] and followed him back to his own ship.

He later purchased the *Bleeding Heart* from the party for 19,000 gold when they upgraded to the *[[Take Heart]]*.

He is part of the [[Emerald Expansion]] enterprise.
