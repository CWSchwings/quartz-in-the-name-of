# Bastian Malivorna

**Species:** Human  
**Affiliation:** [[Malivorna Family]]  
**Location:** [[Durathia]] (Vaelcor's Reach)

---

Bastian is [[Bennett]]'s childhood friend and the scion of a noble family from [[Durathia]]. He and Bennett have maintained a correspondence and the kind of friendship that survives long gaps and longer distances.

He is educated, well-connected, and apparently has tutors he needs to check in with. He keeps seven or more keys hidden behind a false panel in his room, purpose unclear. He did not return to his room on the night the party fought wolves.

He suggested eating in the kitchens to avoid his parents in the dining hall. The dynamics of the Malivorna household are not entirely warm.

## Letters

His correspondence with Bennett is candid. He has offered her a berth in Vaelcor's Reach, access to a ship captain named Vayne for resupply, and an open invitation to visit and rest. He has also expressed concern, in his measured way, that her crew does not fully understand what it means to live with the kind of attention she carries.

He has rejected multiple suitors. He finds the court's candidates uninteresting. He is not rushing.

## Keeping Him Safe

During the wolf hunt, **[[Bennett]]** and Vinnie agreed Bastian should not be in the fight. He does not need to be in the cave.
