# Corren Saithe

**Species:** Human  
**Class:** Mage (Abjuration)  
**Affiliation:** Crew of the [[Bleeding Heart]]  
**Former ship:** [[Moons' Threnody]]

---

## History

Corren was the sole survivor of the *[[Moons' Threnody]]*, a research vessel dedicated to studying magic at sea — far from any population that could be harmed by their experiments. The research eventually turned against them. The magic infected the crew, drove them to fight each other, and Corren was the last one standing. Captain [[Thalia Stormrider]] had been experimenting with an artifact and paid the price.

The party found the ship in [[Session 04 - What's Magics Precious|Session 04]]. They brought Corren aboard and sank the Threnody.

## Aboard the Ship

Corren avoids using magic around others when possible. He is mostly skilled in Abjuration — defensive and containment work. He is aware of the danger magic poses in uncontrolled conditions better than almost anyone still breathing.

During the escape from [[Zhelesyr]], he and a freed tiefling cast Darkness over the ship to break pursuit. It worked.

He was [[Pot]]'s first stop when looking for information about lycanthropy. Corren acknowledged the limits of his knowledge and directed him to [[Thebrin Quass]].
