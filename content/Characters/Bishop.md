# Bishop

**Full name:** p'Neep-rüdab  
**Species:** Bullywug  
**Class:** Artificer  
**Affiliation:** [[Captains]], formerly pirate crew  
**Role aboard ship:** Repairs, construction, engineering

---

## Who He Is

Bishop is mute. His tongue was cut out by his tribe's leader after a falling-out, and he was left for dead. He taught himself to use artisan's tools, built things no one in his tribe expected him to build, and got himself exiled before he was killed.

He escaped with his two closest friends: **[[B'Rieph]]** and **[[Bauble]]**. They found a village, settled in briefly, and were caught in an imperialist raid. Bishop spent three years in forced servitude on a naval vessel. When pirates took that ship, he was picked up as deckhand crew.

He has not seen B'Rieph or Bauble in eight years. He believes they are dead.

*(They are not.)*

## Character

Calculating. Observant. Cheeky when he has a way to say it — signs, gestures, written notes. A sailor's sense of humor. Deeply anti-imperialist in a way that comes from having been on the wrong end of an empire's logistics chain.

He fixates on problems — a project in progress takes over. He drinks. He loves to solve things that shouldn't be solvable.

He was the only one on the dinosaur island who could understand what the [[Calvatharn]] soldiers were saying. He used that knowledge to begin the unraveling of their camp.

## On the Topic of Magic

When the party learned about the mercenary expedition to the New World, Bishop's first instinct was to warn the locals. He was talked into gathering more information first. This is a pattern — he moves toward action faster than the party usually does, but he can be reasoned with.

He is anti-imperial. The [[Order of Bone]] is an institution of control. These facts sit next to each other without having been fully examined yet.
