# Faustus De Lenti

**Species:** Orc  
**Gender:** Male  
**Occupation:** Smith, Gunsmith  
**Affiliation:** [[De Lenti Family]]  
**Location:** [[Draekara]], [[Zhelesyr]]

---

Faustus De Lenti is one of [[Bennett]]'s living parents — a burly orc who works as a smith and gunsmith. He is alive and well.

His family settled in [[Zhelesyr]] after fleeing the fallout of the [[Ember Alliance]]. He and his surviving co-parents opened a smithy in [[Draekara]], where they built a quiet life in the shadow of the fight they left behind.

He is friendly toward Bennett and, by extension, toward her crew.
