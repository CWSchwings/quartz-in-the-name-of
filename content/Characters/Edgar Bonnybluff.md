# Edgar Bonnybluff

**Species:** Halfling (human)  
**Affiliation:** [[Tir Zafiri Navy]]  
**Relation:** [[Johanna]]'s older brother

---

Edgar Bonnybluff is a captain in the [[Tir Zafiri Navy]]. He knew he would be pressed into service eventually and chose to enlist first, on his own terms, with the intent to excel. He arranged for [[Johanna]] to be assigned naval duty rather than imprisonment when she ran out of other options.

He was a kind child. He has grown hard.

When the party visited [[Arinthal Harbor]], Edgar shared what he knew about the mercenary expedition to the New World: the merchant funding it had approached the navy with promises of precious metals and building resources, and the navy declined. The expedition was heading toward an icy region, reportedly to deal with something dangerous.

**[[Rene]]** noticed purple veins on Edgar's skin while he was speaking. Edgar showed no signs of being aware of it.

He is [[Manawracked]]. The cause is unknown.
