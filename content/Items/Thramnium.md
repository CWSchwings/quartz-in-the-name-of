Thramnium is an extremely strong and resilient metal by weight, with the added benefit of a cumulative limited magical corruption resistance (it is able to absorb a certain amount of magical corruption during its lifetime with no ill effect).

Thaumium's creation is a closely guarded secret. It is fabricated only by the [[Order Of Bone]], and within the Order only the [[Order Of Marrow-Bleeding]] are taught the process of crafting it.

A common assumption among others in the order, based on the name, though not confirmed, is that it is somehow crafted of the bones of dead [[Manawracked]].
