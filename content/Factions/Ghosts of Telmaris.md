# Ghosts of Telmaris

**Base:** [[Telmaris]] (sunken city)

---

The Ghosts of Telmaris take their name from the drowned city that serves as their territory. [[Telmaris]] lies beneath the waves — a sunken ruin that the Ghosts have made their own. Whatever remains of the city below provides cover, refuge, and legend in equal measure.

Little is known of their organization, their numbers, or their motives beyond what the name implies: they haunt the waters around Telmaris and consider that drowned place their own.
