# Emerald Expansion

**Type:** Multi-captain shipping enterprise  
**Captains:** [[Zara Firebeard]], [[Dorian Whiteshade]], [[Lysandra Boot]]

---

The Emerald Expansion is a loosely organized group of captains operating together for shared profit. The party encountered them in [[Khareem]] during [[Session 12 - Khareem and the Emerald Expansion|Session 12]].

**[[Zara Firebeard]]** — *[[Celestial Shield]]*, from [[Tir Zafiri]]  
**[[Dorian Whiteshade]]** — *[[Silvered Edge]]*, from [[Zhelesyr]]  
**[[Lysandra Boot]]** — *[[Tulip Blossom]]*, from [[Vallume]]

A deal was made to split proceeds from a found ship with the Expansion during [[Session 14 - Fogwatch Hollow|Session 14]].

Dorian Whiteshade later purchased the *[[Bleeding Heart]]* from the party for 19,000 gold.
