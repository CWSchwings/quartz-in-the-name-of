# Order of Bone

*Magic is a disease. The Order is the Cure.*

---

## What It Is

The Order of Bone is a widespread magic-hunting organization with chapters across [[Arctoriam]]. It is not directly tied to any government, but many governments fund local chapters. It is widely enough known in the Old World that people there simply call it "the Order," assuming no further specification is needed.

Its stated purpose is the elimination of all magic from the world. Its actual operations involve hunting, imprisoning, and killing those who practice magic, those who are [[Manawracked]] by proximity to it, and, in practice, anyone the local chapter decides warrants classification as a threat.

## Internal Structure

The Order presents a unified face externally but is composed of three sub-orders:

| Sub-Order | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| **[[Order of Skull-Gleaming]]** | Unknown |
| **[[Order of Orbital-Unblinking]]** | Unknown |
| **[[Order of Marrow-Bleeding]]** | Creation of [[Thramnium]], the Order's specialized metal |

## Leadership

- **[[Serath Vornea]]** — overall head of the Order
- **[[Jareth Varcryn]]** — head of the [[Zhelesyr]] chapter

## Thramnium

The Order's weapon-crafters produce a metal called **[[Thramnium]]** — extremely strong and resistant to magical corruption. It can absorb a limited amount of corruption over its lifetime. Its creation is known only to the Order of Marrow-Bleeding. Common assumption, unconfirmed, is that it is made from the bones of the [[Manawracked]] dead.

## Known Connections to the Party

- **[[Rene]]** — former member, on the run after his wife [[Evaine]] discovered he had been protecting magic users instead of killing them.
- **[[Bennett]]** — was caught by the Order early in her life and escaped by luck. Their connection to the [[Thalorian Dynasty]] makes them a persistent threat to her family.
- **[[Zhelesyr]] operations** — the party broke out the Order's prisoners and fled under cannon fire. [[Jareth Varcryn]] knows they exist.
