# Calvatharn

**Government:** Technocracy  
**Region:** [[Arctoriam]]  
**Language:** Calvatharn  

---

Calvatharn is a science-forward nation that operates on slave labor. It describes itself as logical. Its opponents describe it as an empire that has outsourced the moral calculation.

The party's first encounter with Calvatharn was on the [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]], where a scientific expedition had set up a camp and outpost using enslaved soldiers to do the dangerous work. The party organized a rebellion, killed the officers, freed the soldiers, and took the ship.

## Current Political Situation

Calvatharn recently formalized an alliance with [[Tir Zafiri]]. The alliance is approximately three to four months old. [[Vallume]] and [[Darathia]] oppose it. Active naval combat has been observed in [[Tir Zafiri]] waters.

## Notable Individuals

- **[[Thebrin Quass]]** — scientist, formerly of the island expedition, now crew of the [[Bleeding Heart]]
- **[[Ciryx Nemetel]]** — Director of Mobile Extraction Operations; [[Bishop]]'s personal nemesis

## Forged Credentials

The party carries a forged [[Calvatharn]] identity: **Senior Metallurgist Ha Zellpen**.
