# Tidebreakers

**Location:** [[Arinthal Harbor]], [[Tir Zafiri]]  
**Leader:** [[Sundrul]]  
**Activities:** Smuggling, protection racket

---

The Tidebreakers are a criminal organization operating out of [[Arinthal Harbor]]. They run a protection racket and handle smuggling through the port. [[Sundrul]], an orc with a soft spot for [[Johanna]], leads them.

[[Johanna]] worked for the Tidebreakers before her naval service, using the connection to purchase protection for her family. That relationship has lasted. Sundrul still provides information and access when the price or the favor is right.

## Current Arrangement

[[Johanna]] has arranged for a keep she controls to serve as a base of operations for the Tidebreakers. **Shadowfin** manages it in her absence and maintains the communication channel.
