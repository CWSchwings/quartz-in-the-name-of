# Castaways of the Unknown Dinosaur Island

**Origin:** [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]]

---

The Castaways of the Unknown Dinosaur Island are a loose group of shipwrecked individuals who arrived on the [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]] before — or independently of — the party. They had established a treetop bungalow hideout by the time the party found them.

When the party formed a plan to liberate the [[Calvatharn]] slaves and steal their anchored ship, the castaways provided support: medical care, supplies, bows, tool kits, and eventually manpower.
