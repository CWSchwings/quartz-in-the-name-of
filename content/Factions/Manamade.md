# Manamade

**Type:** Loose alliance of pro-magic factions

---

The Manamade defy easy categorization. Calling them a group or an organization overstates their cohesion. They are a loose collection of disparate people and cells united by a single thread: a wish to not be killed for their relationship with magic.

Where most of the world understands magic as a dangerous and corrupting force — and where the [[Order of Bone]] hunts practitioners without mercy — the Manamade believe that magic can be understood, controlled, and harnessed. The degree of that belief varies. Some are scholars seeking comprehension. Some are desperate refugees. Some are true believers in magic's potential. What they share is a refusal to accept the death sentence that the wider world has placed on those touched by the arcane.

They have no central leadership, no unified doctrine, no common banner. The only thing that holds them together is the world's hostility toward them.
