# Ember Alliance

**Also known as:** The De Lentis Rebellion  
**Against:** [[Thalorian Dynasty]]  
**Nation:** [[Zhelesyr]]

---

The Ember Alliance was an uprising against the unjust rule of the [[Thalorian Dynasty]]. Its founding leaders — the parents of [[Bennett]] — organized the resistance from within [[Zhelesyr]], drawing together those who had suffered under crown authority.

After years of fighting, the founding family discovered they were expecting a child. Faced with the weight of that future, they made the difficult and divisive decision to abandon the active fight. The choice sparked outrage within the movement. Critics called it a betrayal. Others understood.

The family fled in the dead of night. They were betrayed — their movements reported to the crown. They were ambushed. Some among them gave their lives to ensure the others escaped. [[Bennett]] was born on the ship that carried the survivors out of [[Zhelesyr]].

The suppression of the Ember Alliance did not end the rebellion. The martyrdom of its founders only spread the fire. Further uprisings followed across the kingdom, and the [[Thalorian Dynasty]] has spent years since hunting the surviving [[De Lentis]] family members in an effort to extinguish the legacy entirely.

## De Lentis Family

The founding family of the alliance. Bennett is their only child. Known surviving members:

- **Faustus De Lenti** — orc smith and gunsmith, alive and well
- **Inka De Lenti** — nonbinary human scribe, alive but injured
- **Mayari De Lenti** — elven fighter, alive and well
- **Benicio De Lenti**, **Luca De Lenti**, **Dalisay De Lenti** — additional family members
