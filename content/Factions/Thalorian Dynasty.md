# Thalorian Dynasty

**Nation:** [[Zhelesyr]]  
**Key figure:** Crown Prince [[Vaerik Draevenn]]

---

The ruling family of [[Zhelesyr]]. Crown Prince **[[Vaerik Draevenn]]** is the dynasty's current heir and the nemesis of the **[[De Lentis]]** family.

[[Bennett]]'s parents were founding members of the **[[Ember Alliance]]**, an uprising against Draevenn's unjust rule. The rebellion was quashed — but its suppression sparked further uprisings across the kingdom. The Thalorian crown has been trying to eliminate the remaining De Lentis family members ever since.

The dynasty funds the [[Order of Bone]]'s local chapter through Governor Elrik Thelorian, using it to round up magic users and political dissidents.
