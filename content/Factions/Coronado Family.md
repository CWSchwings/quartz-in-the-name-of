# Coronado Family

**Nation:** [[Tir Zafiri]]  
**Wealth:** Mining rights

---

The Coronado family are a noble house of [[Tir Zafiri]], their fortune built on mining concessions across the nation's holdings. Wealth and standing flow from the rock, not the sea — a distinction that sets them apart from the seafaring merchant families of the coast.

The family produces children in abundance, and inheritance is fiercely contested. Those without obvious talent or claim are expected to forge their own name elsewhere. **[[Isabel Coronado Du Zof]]** — fifth child and captain of the *[[Zns Thalassir]]* — took that expectation seriously. With no path to the family seat, she turned to the navy, intent on winning gold, honors, and a reputation that would demand respect on its own terms.
