# Malivorna Family

**Type:** Noble family  
**Location:** [[Durathia]]

---

The Malivorna Family are aristocrats in [[Durathia]]. **[[Bastian Malivorna]]** is the scion of the family and a childhood friend of [[Bennett]]'s. He maintains their estate — referred to as Vaelcor's Reach — and has extended the party open hospitality there.

The family dynamics are not entirely warm; Bastian prefers eating in the kitchens over joining his parents in the dining hall.
