# Order of Marrow-Bleeding

**Parent Organization:** [[Order of Bone]]  
**Type:** Sub-order

---

The Order of Marrow-Bleeding is one of the internal sub-orders of the [[Order of Bone]]. It is specifically responsible for the creation and handling of **[[Thramnium]]** — the material documented in the party's records.
