# Vallume

**Government:** Monarchy  
**Capital:** [[Vallumos]]  
**Region:** [[Arctoriam]]

---

Vallume is a nation in [[Arctoriam]] that borders [[Kaelmaris]] on the ocean. Its capital is [[Vallumos]].

Queen **[[Ardrae Valthan]]** rules. Her daughter is **[[Elara Valthan]]**.

## Current Affairs

Vallume is at war with [[Tir Zafiri]] and [[Calvatharn]] over their recent military alliance. It is allied with [[Darathia]] in opposition.

Active naval combat between Vallume and Tir Zafiri forces was observed in the harbor when the party returned to Tir Zafiri.

## In the Campaign

The party visited [[Vallumos]], Vallume's capital, and became entangled in the city's politics — including the [[Order of Bone]]'s activities there and a job connected to **[[Serathia Valthan]]**.
