# Seasong Haven

**Type:** Orphanage  
**Location:** [[Arinthal Harbor]], [[Tir Zafiri]]  
**Funding:** Noble patronage

---

Seasong Haven is a noble-funded orphanage in [[Arinthal Harbor]], run by **[[Gloriana Maris]]**. She tried to keep a young [[Caspian]] fed and in lessons when he showed up on her doorstep. He was never one for sitting still.
