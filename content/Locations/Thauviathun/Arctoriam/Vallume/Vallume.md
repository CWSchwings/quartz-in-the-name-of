Nation in centrally location in the main continent of [[Arctoriam]]
# Culture
Image is very important to a Vallumite, as is being seen to be virtuous.
## Etiquette
Disputes are often settled with duels, or at least the challenge of a duel. Often times both parties being willing to duel will avoid the need for one, both being considered to have shown the strength of their convictions. 
## Ideal
The majority of Vallume strives toward valor as their guiding ideal. Most of their heroes are figures that turned the tide of battle. Most of their stories are of those same battles. There is little reverence for great negotiators, great thinkers, or others. 
## People
The average Vallumite is very straightforward, doesn't beat around the bush, and makes their intentions known and their actions direct. 

Every Vallumite of age is prepared for combat at any given moment. After their mandatory miliraty service, it is expected that everyone remain ready to be called back into service, and indeed it is an honor to do so. It is vanishingly rare to meet a Vallumite not wearing heavy armor and carrying a weapon, and all will wear a family crest in some way.

After their military service, most Vallumites go into their family business, or an apprenticeship with another close family. 
# Diplomacy
Vallume takes a supremacist, paternalist stance to diplomacy. They keeping the world's peace as their responsibility, and use their vast and well-trained military to accomplish that. Vallume is of the opionion that the world would be a better place if everyone just followed their lead, as the epitome of that which is good. 

Vallume is allied with [[Durathia]]
# Economy
Vallume's economy is relatively weak due to their size. While they produce an enormous amount, the same amount is tied up in their interventions around the world. They are mostly self-sufficient, and import and export little. 

Vallume is generally a bit technologically stagnated except in military technology. It's not that its people are stupid, just that their brightest minds are often focused on their military.
# Geography
Vallume is vast, and contains most imaginable terrains. It borders [[Kaelmaris]], cutting it off from the ocean. 
# Government
Vallume is an elective absolute monarchy, with all the members of the royal family (and a few influential other families who have somehow earned the privilege) electing a new ruler from amongst themselves when the old ruler dies or abdicates. The old ruler will often indicate a favored successor, and this choice is often, but not always, upheld by the electors. Once elected, the ruler has absolute power.
## Crime
Criminality in Vallume is treated paradoxically. The crackdown on any apparent crime is swift and overwhelming, but guilty criminals who complete their sentence or wrongly accused innocents who can prove that innocence are forgiven as though the offense had never happened. On the other hand, those who attempt to fight to prove their innocence and fail are shunned as having no honor, possibly the worst offense a Vallumite could be accused of. 

The most common legal proceeding is a plea or trial in front of a magistrate, often a distant cousin of the royal family, and then a sentence, often some amount of labor the fruits of which are directed to the aggrieved party. Trial by combat is an option offered to all Vallumites, but most choose to plead their guilt and serve their sentence, knowing that on the other side is reintegration to society. 
## Military
Vallume requires military service from all its citizens from the ages of 15 through 25, starting as a squire and trainee and graduating to a soldier. After this period, the more suited to the task remain in the military, while others leave with their pay from the military ready to start their lifelong profession. All, however, remain ready for combat at any time and regularly drill and train as local militia. Because of this, Vallume maintains the largest militia force the world over, and their army can be focused solely externally.
