# Culture
Seravaxans generally hold the [[Order Of Bone]] in high regard. 
# Geography
Seravax is an archipelago in [[Arctoriam]].  
# Government
There is little central government to speak of in Seravax. Rather, it is a loose collection of monasteries. 
Holds the [[Order Of Bone]] in high regard.
