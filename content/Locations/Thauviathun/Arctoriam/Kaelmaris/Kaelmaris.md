1. It's an extreme authoritarian meritocracy with a dash of eugenics. Isolationist, supremacist but not expansionist. The lack of natural resources and farming means that everyone needs to pull more weight than the average person in another nation. Children and adolescents are "tested" throughout their youth. Things like being left to hunt and gather for themselves in the mountains for a week. Those that don't survive are deemed to have been too weak. Those tests fast-track them in various state determined career paths. Its people are generally hard. Standoffish with outsiders who haven't shown their own merit
    
2. Physically, they tend to be a bit on the large and strong side because those are the ones that survive.
    
3. Competence is a big thing, like honor in Vallume. Adults will voluntarily understand "impossible" tasks as penance if they fuck up real bad. If they succeed, all is forgiven
    
4. Their main industries are artisan and mercenary. Their mercenary units are prized for their effectiveness, but there just aren't enough of them for a standing army
    
5. Nobody wants to attack them though. Their land isn't worth getting into a guerilla battle in the mountains against a bunch of psychos who send their kids into the peaks as childcare over
