# Economy
With their low natural resources, Tir Zafiri largely relies on fishing. Because of their large flotilla of ships, they've gotten an early foothold in the [[Zylandrom|New World]], though they are now straining for manpower.
# Geography
Tir Zafiri is a small nation on the main continent of [[Arctoriam]] between [[Vallume]] and [[Zhelesyr]]. They have relatively few natural resources, beyond easy access to the ocean.
# Government
Tir Zafiri is a Monarchy with an advisory council. In practice, the advisory council makes most of the decisions, with the monarchy acting as a rubber-stamp. In law, the monarchy has absolute power, but rarely chooses to exercise it, preferring to leave the day-to-day concerns to the council as long as everything continues running smoothly.
