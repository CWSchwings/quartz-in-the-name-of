# Ferrakil

**Type:** Moon of [[Thauviathun]]  
**Appearance:** Iron moon  
**Orbital Period:** 4 days

---

Ferrakil is the iron moon of [[Thauviathun]], completing its orbit every four days — the fastest cycle of the three moons.
