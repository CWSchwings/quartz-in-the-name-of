# Vandrossel

**Type:** City  
**Nation:** Kaelmaris  
**Notable:** Primary headquarters of the [[Order of Bone]]

---

Vandrossel is a city in Kaelmaris and the seat of the [[Order of Bone]]'s central authority. Where local chapters operate under governors and regional commanders, the true leadership of the Order is based here. Any significant decision regarding the Order's doctrine, campaigns, or expansion flows from Vandrossel.
