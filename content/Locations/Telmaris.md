# Telmaris

**Type:** Sunken city  
**Region:** [[Arctoriam]] (submerged)

---

Telmaris is a sunken city. The [[Ghosts of Telmaris]] are associated with it — travelers, spirits, or something else entirely, connected to what it was before it went under.

The party arrived in [[Session 20 - Telmaris|Session 20]] and began exploring the ruins. **[[Sszraak]]** was found floating on a raft nearby.

The **[[Tidal Obelisk]]** beacon points to a location relevant to Telmaris.
