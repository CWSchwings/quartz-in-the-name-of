# Unknown Dinosaur Island

**Region:** New World ([[Zylandrom]])  
**Status:** No official name assigned

---

An uncharted island in the waters of [[Zylandrom]], the New World. No one who has been there has given it a name. The parties involved were mostly focused on surviving it.

## Geography

- **East:** Vast sandflats, open and exposed.
- **West:** Dense jungle.
- **North:** Jungle leading to an offshore anchorage point used by the [[Calvatharn]] expedition.
- **Interior:** River approximately two hours inland. Elevated terrain toward the center, mountain with nesting flying species at the peak.
- **Treetop level:** Former dwelling of a group of castaways who had been there long enough to build.

## Inhabitants

The island hosts a variety of large reptilian fauna — velociraptors, deinonychus, frilled deathspitters, carnosaurs, an uncatalogued plesiosaur in the river, and a young blue dragon in the offshore waters. Herbivore species can be persuaded to tolerate humans. Predators cannot.

## The Calvatharn Presence

A [[Calvatharn]] scientific expedition had established a camp and outpost on the island. They were attempting to recover dinosaur eggs and other biological samples. Their soldiers were, by and large, enslaved people who had been given no choice in the matter.

The party coordinated a rebellion, killed the officers, and liberated the soldiers. They took the ship.

## Who Made It Off

The [[Captains]] and their ship's complement, including:
- Surviving slave-soldiers and miners who chose to join the crew
- The treetop castaway group (some departed in [[Zhelesyr]], some stayed)
- [[Thebrin Quass]], the [[Calvatharn]] scientist

The island itself remains. The [[Calvatharn]] camp is destroyed. The [[Order of Bone]] does not yet know what happened there. A merchant expedition is reportedly heading to the New World with a large number of ships. Their destination is described as icy rather than tropical, but the existence of the island is no longer unknown to as many people as it once was.
