# Thalassir

**Type:** Moon of [[Thauviathun]]  
**Appearance:** Shimmering blue with visible swirling currents  
**Orbital Period:** 13 days

---

Thalassir is the water moon of [[Thauviathun]]. It appears as a blue orb with currents visibly moving across its surface.
