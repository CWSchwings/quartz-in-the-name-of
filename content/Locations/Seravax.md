# Seravax

**Type:** Monastic archipelago  
**Region:** [[Arctoriam]]

---

Seravax is a monastic archipelago in [[Arctoriam]]. It holds the [[Order of Bone]] in high regard, making it a location of particular significance in the Order's broader cultural and theological reach.
