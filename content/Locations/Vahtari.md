# Vahtari

**Type:** City  
**Region:** [[Zylandrom]] (mountains)

---

Vahtari is a city in the mountains of [[Zylandrom]], the New World. It was the intended destination of **[[Zhessir Kraakel]]** and the group of castaways stranded on the [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]] — people looking for somewhere better, who had not yet found it.
