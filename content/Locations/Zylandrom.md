# Zylandrom

**Type:** Continent — the New World  
**Part of:** [[Thauviathun]]

---

Zylandrom is the New World — a largely unmapped continent on the other side of the ocean from [[Arctoriam]]. It is home to dinosaurs, indigenous species, and as yet unclaimed territory by the nations of Arctoriam, though expeditions are ongoing and competition for footholds is intensifying.

The **[[Unknown Dinosaur Island]]** is an island in the waters associated with this region. The city of **[[Vahtari]]** lies in the mountains of Zylandrom.

Several factions from Arctoriam — including [[Calvatharn]] and the [[Emerald Expansion]] — have been mounting expeditions into Zylandrom.
