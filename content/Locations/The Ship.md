## History

The ship was a [[Calvatharn]] scientific expedition vessel. It was anchored off the [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]] when the party arrived. The crew on board was [[Thebrin Quass]]; everyone else had gone ashore. The party took it after the island rebellion succeeded, answered the ship's flare signal incorrectly, watched it try to leave, and had [[Pot]] swim out to intercept.

## Crew

The **[[Crew]]** is a large, polyglot group assembled from:
- Liberated slave-soldiers from [[Calvatharn]]
- Treetop castaways from the island
- Sailors recruited or acquired in [[Zhelesyr]] and [[Tir Zafiri]]
- Freed prisoners from [[Zhelesyr]]'s jail (tieflings, bullywugs, lizardfolk)
- Sundry others taken on in port

At last count: approximately fifty total. Twenty departed in [[Zhelesyr]] with severance pay.

The named lizardfolk crew members are: Bernadette, Olivia, Francesco, Arthur, Frankie, Boga, Erick, Sergio, Zachariah, Chace, Amos, Flowers, Logan, Silver, Ozzie, Jagger, Scottie, Purdy, Skipper, Darwin, Bully, Friday, Wesley, Nutmeg, Gretel, Blanche, Flake, Bosco, Beauty, and Curly.
