# Arinthal Harbor

**Nation:** [[Tir Zafiri]]  
**Type:** Major port city

---

## Underworld

The Tir Zafiri criminal underworld in Arinthal Harbor operates through five major figures:

1. **Lady Varissa "The Whispercloak" Darnel** — half-elf, espionage and intelligence
2. **"Iron-Tooth" Garric Thane** — dwarf, smuggling and artifact recovery
3. **Silk-Finger Nali** — human, pickpocketing and infiltration
4. **The Jackal Prince** — unknown race, assassinations
5. **Old Mother Quent** — gnome, poisons and information brokerage

The **[[Tidebreakers]]** operate here. [[Sundrul]] leads them and has a soft spot for [[Johanna]].

## Political Notes

The harbor sees significant naval traffic. The [[Tir Zafiri Navy]] pressgangs from the local population. The **Mandic family** led the coalition to ally with [[Calvatharn]]. **[[Dashiell Howell]]** dissented against that alliance.
