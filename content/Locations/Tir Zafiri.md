# Tir Zafiri

**Government:** Monarchy with advisory council  
**Region:** [[Arctoriam]], between [[Vallume]] and [[Zhelesyr]]

---

A small nation with limited natural resources. Historically fishing-based, which gave it an early start in New World exploration. Currently hurting for manpower.

## Arinthal Harbor

**[[Arinthal Harbor]]** is the largest port in [[Tir Zafiri]]. The party docked there in [[Session 08 - Incognito Mode|Session 08]] under the ship's new name *[[Bleeding Heart]]*. Notable locations:

- **[[Salty Gull]]** — dive bar, [[Johanna]]'s contact [[Calvados]] works here
- **[[Rusty Anchor]]** — tavern for retired sailors
- **[[Seasong Haven]]** — noble-funded orphanage, run by Gloriana Maris

## The Navy

[[Tir Zafiri]] maintains its navy largely through pressganging — pulling locals into service. Unaffiliated ships in harbor are read as potential threats, which explains why the local population tends to disappear when strangers dock. The navy is commanded partly by the [[Coronado Family]], a noble house that acquires wealth through mining rights.

[[Caspian]] and [[Johanna]] both served in this navy under duress. Their former ship, the *Zns Thalassir*, was captained by **[[Isabel Coronado Du Zof]]**. The actual power aboard was the first mate, **[[Sloane]]**, who ran a wage-skimming "savings program" for crew members who had no power to refuse.

## Current Political Situation

Three to four months before the party's return in the [[Session XX - Return to Tir Zafiri|later sessions]], [[Tir Zafiri]] formalized an alliance with [[Calvatharn]]. [[Vallume]] and [[Darathia]] oppose it. Active naval combat was observed in the harbor on arrival.

**Dashiell Howell** is a high-ranking [[Tir Zafiri]] official opposed to the [[Calvatharn]] alliance. He may be a useful contact.
