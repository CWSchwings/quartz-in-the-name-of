# Vallumos

**Type:** Capital city

The capital of [[Vallume]]. The party arrived aboard the *[[Take Heart]]* and gave dockmaster **[[Lucius Stormrider]]** the ship's new name.

## Politics

[[Vallume]] is currently at war with [[Tir Zafiri]] and [[Calvatharn]] over their new alliance. Queen **Ardrae Valthan** rules. Her daughter is **Elara Valthan**.

**Alden Valthan** was taken by the [[Order of Bone]] during the party's visit.

**Caldrin Valthan** is the target of a job the party accepted from **[[Serathia Valthan]]**.

The party had coordinates for a [[Vallume]] princess as of [[Session 13 - The Open Water|Session 13]].
