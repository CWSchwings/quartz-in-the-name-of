# Khareem

**Nation:** [[Tir Zafiri]]  
**Type:** Major port city

---

One of the largest port cities in [[Tir Zafiri]]. The party stopped here during [[Session 12 - Khareem and the Emerald Expansion|Session 12]].

**Notable locations:**
- **Restful Skies** — inn where the party met **[[Zara Firebeard]]** and the [[Emerald Expansion]]

Eight shipwrecks were floating in nearby waters during the party's visit. The ***[[Blue Leviathan]]*** was also spotted and avoided.
