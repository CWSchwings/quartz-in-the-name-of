# Zhelesyr

**Government:** Monarchy under the [[Thalorian Dynasty]]  
**Region:** [[Arctoriam]] (Old World)

---

A kingdom governed by the Thalorian crown. The ruling dynasty is the [[Thalorian Dynasty]], currently represented by Crown Prince **[[Vaerik Draevenn]]**, who the party has reason to regard as an enemy.

## Ports

- **[[Mirathis Harbor]]** — largest port in [[Zhelesyr]]
- **[[Draekara]]** — city where [[Bennett]]'s family settled and opened their smithy

## Political Climate

The [[Order of Bone]] has a chapter in [[Zhelesyr]], headed by **[[Jareth Varcryn]]**. The Order has an arrangement with **Governor Elrik Thelorian** and **Captain of the Guard Thaylord**: money changes hands and people suspected of magic are delivered to the Order's custody. The city's prison filled with tieflings, bullywugs, and lizardfolk while the party was in port.

The party broke them out and escaped under cannon fire. They are not welcome back.

## The Ember Alliance

**[[Bennett]]'s** parents were founding members of an uprising against Crown Prince Vaerik Draevenn. The Ember Alliance came apart, sparked further rebellions anyway, and the surviving family has been running from Thalorian reach ever since. The movement is part of [[Zhelesyr]]'s history, still contested.
