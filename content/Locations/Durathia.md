# Durathia

**Region:** [[Arctoriam]]  
**Affiliation:** Home of the [[Malivorna Family]]

---

A nation in [[Arctoriam]] where the [[Malivorna Family]] holds aristocratic standing. **[[Bastian Malivorna]]** resides here, at **Vaelcor's Reach** — which is both a port district and the name associated with the Malivorna estate.

The party visited during [[Session 30 - Durathia|Session 30]] and [[Session 34 - 35 - Vaelcor's Reach Again|Sessions 34–35]].

**[[Bennett]]** was knighted by **Affraic Malivorna** during [[Session 40 - 42 - Back to Tir Zafiri|Sessions 40–41]].
