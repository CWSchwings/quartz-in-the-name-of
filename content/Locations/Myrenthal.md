# Myrenthal

**Government:** Unknown  
**Region:** [[Arctoriam]]  
**Notable feature:** Rival of [[Calvatharn]]

---

A nation in [[Arctoriam]] with an established shipping guild and significant trade infrastructure. The party has passed through multiple times, and [[Myrenthal]] has been a staging point for several voyages.

## Vaelcor's Reach

A port city in [[Myrenthal]], associated with [[Bastian Malivorna]]'s family. The **Malivorna** estate is nearby. Notable contacts there:
- **[[Sereth Kall]]**, the Veilblade
- **[[Saedrien Moth]]** and apprentice [[Kadin Vonderry]]
- **Doran Corvath**, encountered at a dinner of captains

The **Red Birch Tavern** operates in Vaelcor's Reach.

## The Shipping Guild

The *[[Bleeding Heart]]* enrolled in the **Myrenthal Shipping Guild** for 30 gold, valid for approximately one year. This membership provided access to transport contracts and legitimacy in port.

## Forged Credentials

Party carries forged [[Myrenthal]] identity: **Cryptographers' Guild Deputy Head Sesklos Adalwen**.

## Calvatharn Blockade

A [[Calvatharn]] blockade was detected near [[Myrenthal]] waters during [[Session 22 - The Passage to Myrenthal|Session 22]]. Connected to their search for **[[Myara Varris]]**.
