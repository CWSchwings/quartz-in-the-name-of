# Arctoriam

**Type:** Continent — the Old World  
**Part of:** [[Thauviathun]]

---

Arctoriam is the Old World — the continent where the party originates. It contains the majority of the known nations they have dealt with: [[Zhelesyr]], [[Tir Zafiri]], [[Vallume]], [[Calvatharn]], [[Myrenthal]], [[Kaelmaris]], [[Darathia]], and [[Durathia]].

The monastic archipelago of **[[Seravax]]** sits in Arctoriam and holds the [[Order of Bone]] in high regard.

Arctoriam stands in contrast to **[[Zylandrom]]**, the New World — a largely unmapped continent that has become the focus of colonial expeditions from Arctoriam's nations.
