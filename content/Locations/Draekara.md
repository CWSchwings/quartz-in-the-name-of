# Draekara

**Type:** City  
**Nation:** [[Zhelesyr]]

---

A city within [[Zhelesyr]]. The De Lentis family — [[Bennett]]'s parents — hail from Draekara. After fleeing the collapse of the [[Ember Alliance]], the surviving family members eventually settled here, opening a smithy to support themselves while remaining within reach of the country they once tried to change.
