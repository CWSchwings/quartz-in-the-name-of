# Thistlebrook

**Type:** Small settlement

---

A small town the party passed through during [[Session 17 - Underwater Temple|Sessions 17]] and [[Session 18 - Thistlebrook|18]].

**Willow** — a herbalist with a love for Thistlebrook's natural beauty — was elected townmaster in a local election observed by the party. [[Caspian]] gave her 100 gold.
