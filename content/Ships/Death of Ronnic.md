# Death of Ronnic

**Affiliation:** Pirate  
**Flag:** Black squid with a single white eye on a blue field

---

A pirate vessel known by its distinctive flag — a black squid, one eye rendered in white, set against deep blue. The name and the banner both suggest a history of violence and deliberate menace.

Little else is recorded of the ship or those who sail her.
