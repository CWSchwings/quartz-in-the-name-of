# Khalvyn's Sacrifice

**Status:** Renamed — now the *[[Bleeding Heart]]*

---

The *Khalvyn's Sacrifice* was the party's original ship, taken from the [[Calvatharn]] expedition on the [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]] after liberating the enslaved crew. The party renamed her the *[[Bleeding Heart]]* when they arrived at [[Arinthal Harbor]] and needed to travel incognito.
