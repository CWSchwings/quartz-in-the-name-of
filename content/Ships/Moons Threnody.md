# Moons' Threnody

**Captain:** [[Thalia Stormrider]] (Constellate Archer)  
**Purpose:** Magic research vessel

---

The *Moons' Threnody* was a research vessel dedicated to the study of magic. Its crew chose to conduct their work far out to sea, keeping their experiments away from populated shores where an accident could harm innocents.

The precaution was not enough. Eventually, the research turned against them. The crew bludgeoned one another to death in what survivors describe only as a catastrophic loss of control. **[[Corren Saithe]]** was the sole survivor of the event.

The ship's fate stands as a warning of what uncontrolled magical study can produce — and what the [[Manamade]]'s ambitions risk repeating.
