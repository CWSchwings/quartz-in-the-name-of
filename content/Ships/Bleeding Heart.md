# Bleeding Heart

**Formerly:** *[[Khalvyn's Sacrifice]]*  
**Affiliation:** [[Captains]]

The *Bleeding Heart* is the renamed *[[Khalvyn's Sacrifice]]* — the party's ship, given a new name when they arrived at [[Arinthal Harbor]] under [[Session 08 - Incognito Mode|Session 08]] to avoid recognition.

The ship has gone by multiple names across the campaign as circumstances have required.
