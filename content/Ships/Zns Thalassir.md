# Zns Thalassir

**Captain:** [[Isabel Coronado Du Zof]]  
**First Mate:** [[Sloane]] ("The Shark")  
**Nationality:** [[Tir Zafiri]]

---

The *Zns Thalassir* is the vessel commanded by **[[Isabel Coronado Du Zof]]**, fifth child of the [[Coronado Family]] and a captain driven by ambition rather than birthright. She took command to build a name for herself — no inheritance awaited her at home, so she would earn distinction at sea.

The true power below deck belongs to **[[Sloane]]**, her first mate. A greasy, calculating opportunist known as "The Shark," Sloane is the reason much of the crew is aboard at all — he haunts the docks, preying on drunkards and the down-on-their-luck at gambling tables, then forces them onto the ship to work off debts. He runs a "savings program" that skims wages from new recruits under the pretense of financial responsibility. The common crew despise him, but he has packed every ranked position with his own loyalists, ensuring compliance.

[[Johanna]] served aboard this vessel before the campaign began. [[Caspian]] also has history with the ship and its captain.
