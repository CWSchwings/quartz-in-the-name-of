# Graven Deep

**Status:** Former vessel

---

The *Graven Deep* was the ship that marooned **[[Tomal Cray]]** after deciding he gossiped too much. He had funded a voyage to the New World to establish a trading outpost and paid for his passage with his own money, but the crew left him stranded regardless.

The party encountered Tomal adrift and brought him aboard, where he proved useful as a linguist.
