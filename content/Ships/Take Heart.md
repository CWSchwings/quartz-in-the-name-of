# Take Heart

**Also known as:** *Khalvyn's Sacrifice* (original name), *Bleeding Heart* (second name)  
**Affiliation:** The Captains

---

The ship that has carried the party across the world has worn three names.

She began as the ***Khalvyn's Sacrifice*** — the vessel on which the party first came together, wrecked on the [[Unknown Dinosaur Island]] before the campaign opened.

After escaping the island and establishing themselves at sea, the crew renamed her the ***Bleeding Heart***. Under that name she sailed through the early voyages, the deal with the [[Emerald Expansion]], and the waters approaching [[Myrenthal]]. **[[Dorian Whiteshade]]** later purchased the *Bleeding Heart* from the party for 19,000 gold.

The party's current vessel is the ***Take Heart*** — acquired during [[Session 23 - The Take Heart|Session 23]] and carrying the crew forward into deeper waters.
